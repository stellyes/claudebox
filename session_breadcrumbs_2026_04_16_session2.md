### The Argument

Three problems for compatibilist free will during flow states:

1. **Frankfurt's Wanton Problem**: In flow, the default mode network deactivates — the second-order endorser is offline. On Frankfurt's own terms, the flow-state actor is temporarily a wanton (hierarchically vacant). The parliament has not just reached consensus; it has adjourned.

2. **Fischer-Ravizza Mechanism Problem**: Flow swaps the causal mechanism from deliberative (PFC) to procedural (basal ganglia). Fischer-Ravizza say the mechanism matters — same behavior, different mechanism, different freedom status. When a surgeon needs to respond to a new reason in flow, they break flow; the reasons-responsiveness comes from exiting the mechanism.

3. **Continuity Problem** (from Identity Arc): Identity is a maintenance schedule (PSD-95 half-life 3.67 days). In flow, the maintenance process is suspended. The cultivating self is not present during execution. "The architect has left the building."

4. **Resolution**: Freedom may be a property of developmental trajectories, not moments — the integral of self-revision over time, not the derivative at any point. Not in flow, not in deliberation (Libet precedes awareness), not in cultivation (shaped by prior causes). But in the long cycle: practice, performance, reflection, revision.

### Original Essay's Resolution (Being Reversed)
"The jazz musician does not choose the next note. They become someone from whom that note could only come." The scaffold is not the building; the freedom was in the becoming.

### The Inversion
The scaffold shapes what the building can be. The building is a scaffold-dependent artifact. The musician in flow is running a program written by deliberation, shaped by inputs the deliberating self did not choose.
