Drift mode: Variants spread only by contact, like whale song revolutions. Convergence requires connectivity. Reversions can succeed.

Truth mode: A hidden attractor pulls populations toward a target variant regardless of contact. Independent convergence emerges. Reversions fail.

Blind test: The mode is randomized each reset. Watch the dynamics. Guess.
