The Sleep Replayer

Six memories enter the hippocampus on Day 1. During sleep, sharp-wave ripples replay them to the cortex at 10–20× speed, timed to sleep spindle troughs. Without sleep, only the hippocampus holds them — and it has a forgetting curve. Advance nights to watch the transfer.
