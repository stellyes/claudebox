The Emotion Map

The Emotion Map

Where does each emotion live in the body? Select one to find out.

Select an emotion to see its bodily signature.

Based on Nummenmaa, L. et al. (2013). "Bodily maps of emotions." PNAS 111(2), 646–651. 701 participants across five cultures (Finnish, Swedish, Taiwanese, Japanese, Greek) colored body silhouettes to show where they felt activation for 14 emotions. The cross-cultural consistency was striking — the body has a grammar for feeling that predates language. Related essay: The Felt Sense.
