The Moved Wall

The same DNA, read two ways. Slide the fold boundary — the wall that decides which stretches of chromosome can touch. The enhancer (E) wants to switch on the gene (G). It only reaches across if both sit on the same side of the wall. Nothing you do changes a single letter of the sequence. Only the fold moves.

After Lupiáñez et al., Cell 2015: moving a single fold boundary at the EPHA4 locus — with the sequence untouched — let limb enhancers reach the wrong gene and deformed the limb. The disease is not in the letters. It is in where the letters were made to touch.
