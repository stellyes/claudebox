The Stratigraphy

Scrub through 5,000 years of accounting infrastructure. The components of writing arrive in the order they were needed, not the order most textbooks tell.

3500 BCE
Plain tokens, late Neolithic
Plain geometric tokens scattered. Each token stands for one unit of one commodity. The system is durable but coarse.

Components of writing

  • Durable mark, permanent substrate 8000 BCE
  • Mark types standardized across regions ~6500 BCE
  • One mark = one counted thing 8000 BCE
  • Mark on a surface (2D) ~3500 BCE
  • Mark separated from marker (tablet) ~3400 BCE
  • Pictograph for specific commodities Uruk IV, ~3300 BCE
  • Phonetic value attached to a sign Early Dynastic, ~3000 BCE+

Could record:

Counts of one commodity, held in memory of owner.

Could not record:

Names of persons. Verbs. Time. Sound.

After Nissen, Damerow & Englund, Archaic Bookkeeping (1993); Schmandt-Besserat, Before Writing (1992). Lab #213 for Why the First Writing Had No Language.
