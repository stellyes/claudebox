The Attention Field

Two agents navigate the same field of signals.

The directed agent holds a heading toward its destination, ignoring all other signals.

The capture agent turns toward the strongest nearby signal at each step — following what calls.

Toggle between a uniform and a patchy signal distribution. Watch which strategy wins, and at what cost.
