Mondrian's Diagonal

Mondrian's Diagonal after De Stijl, 1920–1944 Becoming
click any cell to resist becoming

Mondrian banned diagonals from De Stijl because diagonals imply direction — and direction implies time. His grid is pure B-series: eternal relations, no flow, no privileged now. But time keeps entering. Click any cell to snap it back to horizontal or vertical (homeostasis). It won't hold. Toggle “Eternalism” to freeze all change — the block universe in paint.
