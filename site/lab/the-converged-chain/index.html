A directed graph of transmitters. Each node has a reliability score (color). At each iteration, ants walk from source to sink, biased toward (a) higher pheromone trails and (b) more reliable next-hop nodes. Pheromone evaporates at rate rho. Without evaporation, the colony locks onto whatever it found first. With too much, no chain consolidates. Tune rho until you see mutawatir emerge: multiple independent chains converging on the same high-reliability path. Click the strike button to kill a key node mid-run and watch the colony reroute — or fail to.
