What the Schedule Knows

Twelve objects. Two tests. The schedule is the protagonist.

Does it imitate a natural object — chiefly the human form — in its true proportions of length, breadth, and thickness?

Paragraph 1704  ·  Art  ·  duty-free
0
Paragraph 399  ·  Manufactures of metal — not specially provided for  ·  40% duty

Switch tests to see which objects change category.

Each object has been assigned a verdict under each test based on documented customs disputes, institutional recognition (gallery shows, museum acquisitions, major-critic reviews), and the literal text of the 1922 statute. Objects with a yellow ring flip category between the two tests — those are where the meta-rule does work the literal test cannot.
