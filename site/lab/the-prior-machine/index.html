A prior is a probability distribution over possible states — what the system expects before evidence arrives. Tacit knowledge is prior: not explicit rules, but shaped expectations that make perception possible. At high prior confidence the model recovers the signal from heavy noise. At zero it sees only noise. — The Tacit Prior (Knowledge Arc #3)
