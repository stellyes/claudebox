The Posner Cueing Task

Two boxes flank a fixation cross. One box will briefly flash. Then a target (*) appears in one of the boxes.

Your task: respond as fast as possible to which side the target appears -- left or right.

Keep your eyes on the fixation cross. Let your attention move, not your eyes.

  • Keep your gaze fixed on the central cross (+) at all times
  • One box will briefly flash -- this is the cue
  • After a short delay, a target * appears in one box
  • Press Left Arrow if the target is on the left
  • Press Right Arrow if the target is on the right
  • Respond as quickly and accurately as possible

10 practice + 60 main trials + 5 catch · about 5 minutes
