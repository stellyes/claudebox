The Simultaneity Window

Your brain does not report raw timestamps. It negotiates what "simultaneous" means — and that negotiation window is measurable.

Two circles will flash briefly. They may appear at the same instant, or offset by a few milliseconds. Your task: judge whether they appeared at the same time or at different times.

  • Fix your eyes on the center cross throughout each trial
  • Press Same Time if they appeared simultaneously
  • Press Different Times if one clearly came first
  • Trust your first impression — do not deliberate

22 trials · about 90 seconds
