The Costless Signal

An identity claim is tested by three honesty mechanisms. The body supplies all three for free. Strip the body and watch how long a lie survives.

Index
welded to the body — cannot be faked
Handicap
too costly to fake for long
Deterrent
caught later if incoherent
Choose a channel and run. In person, an embodied lie is caught at once. In text, only coherence can betray it — slowly.

Calibrated so that a large lie told by a skilled deceiver over a text-only channel survives on the order of the real two years the “electronic lover” persona lasted. Read the essay: The Body Was the Password.
