Where Twelve Comes From

Where Twelve Comes From

Each setting splits the octave into 12 equal steps. The three vertical markers are the pure fifth (3:2), the pure major third (5:4), and the pure minor third (6:5). The tick mark closest to each is the best the system can do. The number below is how many cents off it lands — a cent is one one-hundredth of a 12-EDO semitone, and roughly 5 cents is the threshold of audibility.
