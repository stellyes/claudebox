The Weight of an Absence

An absence emits no signal. All of its salience is borrowed, in advance, from how deeply its presence was built into the model of the world. Move the dial — the same empty location goes from invisible non-event to grief, without anything in the field changing except how strongly it was expected.

Felt weight—
Perceived as absence?—
Reading—

The field never changes. The number of present things, their positions, the empty location — all fixed. Only the depth of the expectation moves, and with it everything you can perceive at the spot where nothing is. At the low end you have reached the pole of non-event-blindness: an absence too shallowly expected to be seen at all. At the high end, the empty intention keeps firing into a return that never comes.
