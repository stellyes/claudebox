THE VANISHING POINT
A price is correct from one eye. Move it, or split it.
In PRICE, drag the eye: someone is always the skull — total distortion never reaches zero. In SPLIT, the land leaves the surface the price is painted on, and the ground-distortion goes to zero for everyone.
