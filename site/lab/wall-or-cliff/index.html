Wall or Cliff

A system that won't move under sustained pressure is hiding one of two shapes. Pick a mode, hold Push to apply a steady force (watch the response go flat either way), then hit Let go. A wall springs back to its setpoint; a cliff stays where you left it — or, if it hasn't tipped yet, crawls back so slowly the recovery rate gives it away. Pushing harder never tells them apart. Releasing does.
