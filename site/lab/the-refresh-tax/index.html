The Refresh Tax

The Refresh Tax

Two ways to hold a stock against decay. Hot storage refreshes every cell every tick — the bill scales with how much you keep. Cold storage charges only when you reach for something, but each retrieval is expensive (sequence the pool, mount the tape). Pick a tier, set the stock, the access rate, and the cost of a cold read, then run the clock and watch the two bills diverge.
