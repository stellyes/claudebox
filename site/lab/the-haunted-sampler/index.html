The Haunted Sampler

The Haunted Sampler

Two samplers, same drive, same seed, same temperature. The left one leaves a fading inhibition on every alternative it didn’t pick — a negative-priming residue. The right one discards the unselected entirely. The token timelines diverge because of what the unselected does to the next step.
