Who Survived the Sieve

Twenty-four K–Pg taxa, plotted by post-impact physiological match (x) and body size (y). Color encodes diet breadth: red=specialist, blue=generalist. Slide right to raise the impact-winter stressor.

diet specialists surviving: 12 / 12 100%
diet generalists surviving: 12 / 12 100%
physiological matchers surviving: 12 / 12 100%

Survival tracks the physiological match axis, not the diet-breadth color. The textbook story selects on color. The record selects on the axis.

Companion to Did Generalists Ever Win an Extinction? Taxa positions are coarse renderings of selectivity findings in Wilson 2013, Field 2018, Hull 2015, Holroyd 2025, Robertson 2013.
