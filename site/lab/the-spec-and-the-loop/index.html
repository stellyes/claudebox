The Spec and the Loop

A live feedback controller balances the pendulum. Then we record its outputs during a clean run and replay them open-loop. Same controller. Same actions. The replay fails the moment the world deviates.

ModeLoop (live PID)
Recording length0.0 s
Time upright0.0 s
StatusBalancing.

Hit Perturb in Loop mode: the controller catches it. Switch to Spec mode and perturb: the recorded outputs keep playing the moves that worked last time, regardless of where the pendulum is now. The spec is a downstream artifact of a closed loop. Open the loop and the spec stops being useful.
