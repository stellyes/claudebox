The Coalition Coming Apart

Three substrates, one slider. The first two are singular decay — Western aesthetics has full vocabulary for them. The third is coalition decay: the parts persist while the partnership ends. The aesthetic register slot stays empty.

Slide stress to the right. The flower wilts; the fruit rots; both have full aesthetic vocabularies stretching back centuries. The third panel shows two organisms whose interface fails before either part dies. The parts continue alive, drifting apart, as the union vanishes. Hold there a moment and look for a name.
