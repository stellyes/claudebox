The Trace Twin

Three knots. Eight invariants. Classical tools cannot distinguish the Conway knot from its mutant. Piccirillo's witness fires the one invariant that matters and inherits the answer through the trace.

Conway knot

K_C · 11n34 · 11 crossings
Topologically slice (Freedman 1982). Smoothly slice status: open until 2020.

Kinoshita-Terasaka knot

K_KT · 11n42 · 11 crossings
Mutant of K_C. Smoothly slice. Discovered Kinoshita-Terasaka 1957.

Piccirillo's witness

K_P · constructed 2018 · ~30 crossings
Built by annulus twist on K_C. Shares zero-trace X_0 with K_C.
