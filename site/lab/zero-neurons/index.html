How the brain encodes nothing as something

Neuron firing rates (prefrontal cortex)
The zero neuron fires most strongly. The brain does not register nothing as silence — it actively signals the empty set.
Based on the work of Andreas Nieder (University of Tübingen), whose recordings from macaque prefrontal and parietal cortex identified neurons tuned to specific numerosities — including zero.
