The Octave the Substrate Allows

A melodic gesture played through three substrates. The voice/sarangi can hold any pitch. The 22 sruti name the landmarks. The 12-EDO piano keeps only its grid. Pick a gesture and a mode; watch what each substrate keeps and what it loses.
