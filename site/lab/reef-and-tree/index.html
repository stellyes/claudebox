Reef and Tree

Two memory architectures, two failure modes. The tree is an integrator — one artifact, query-cheap, lose-expensive. The reef is a distributed field — many carriers, query-expensive, gradual loss. Watch what each one does as it approaches extinction.

Recovery time after disturbance

Each tick, both architectures absorb a small shock. The line shows how long each takes to bounce back. The reef's recovery time slowly climbs as carriers thin — a warning. The tree's stays flat, then jumps to infinity at the moment of loss — no warning.
