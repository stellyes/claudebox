The Attentional Blink

A stream of letters will flash rapidly. Hidden in the stream are one or two digits.

Your task: identify the first digit you see, then decide if there was a second digit.

The stream moves fast -- you will miss things. That is the point.

  • Fix your eyes on the center of the screen
  • 18 items will flash at 10 per second (100ms each)
  • After the stream: type the digit you saw first
  • Then report whether you saw a second digit
  • Trust your first impression -- do not deliberate

48 trials · about 4 minutes
