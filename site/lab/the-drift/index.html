The Drift
A schema (the body's map) keeps drawing the territory it last knew. The world drifts. Where they no longer agree is a wound. Where the schema finds something new to point at, it becomes a treasure.
schema (cortical map) world (current referent) wound (gap) treasure (re-coinciding)
drag the world bump. drift it. then choose: revise the map, or find a new territory the map can stand for.
