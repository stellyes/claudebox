The Sandpile

THE RULE
When a cell accumulates 4 or more grains, it topples: redistributes 1 grain to each of its 4 neighbors. Grains at the boundary fall off.
That is the entire model.
CONTROLS
Speed 10/s
AVALANCHE SIZE DISTRIBUTION
After ~1000 avalanches, the distribution converges to a power law (slope −1 on log-log axes). This is the SOC signature: no characteristic scale.
