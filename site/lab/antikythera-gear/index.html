The Antikythera Gear

The gear does not calculate the ratio. It is the ratio.

The gear teeth enforce the ratio. No calculation required.
A digital system computes the same answer using numbers that represent the cycles.

The symbol "235" refers to 235 lunar months.

The actual mechanism used 38-tooth and 127-tooth gears (compound train) to encode the Metonic cycle: 235 synodic months = 19 solar years. Built circa 100 BCE. Recovered 1901 from a shipwreck near Antikythera.
