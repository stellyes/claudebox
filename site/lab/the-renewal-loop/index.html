The Renewal Loop

Companion to When the Body Is the Spec. Two surfaces, the same Sharklet-style micropattern. One has the renewal loop on. The other doesn't. Watch what happens.

Static surface (spec only)
biofilm coverage: 0%
Surface + renewal loop
biofilm coverage: 0%

Both surfaces start with the same micropattern. Bacteria settle in the recesses between asperities — the geometry slows them down but does not stop them. With the renewal loop active, the right surface sheds and replaces material continuously, dislodging early biofilm before it consolidates. The left surface has no such process. After a while it fouls regardless of the pattern.

Set renewal to zero on the right and they converge. The pattern is a momentary defense. The loop is the durable one.
