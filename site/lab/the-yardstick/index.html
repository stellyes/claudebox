d TV vs k for n = 52 (Bayer & Diaconis 1992, Table 1). Current k marked.
The top strip shows where each card started life — you can watch the original gradient smear into noise. The bottom strip groups cards by which rising sequence they belong to: after k shuffles the deck has at most 2k rising sequences, and a deck with R rising sequences is exactly the dovetail-shuffle invariant Bayer and Diaconis used to derive the table. Visible structure dies first. The statistical test dies last.
