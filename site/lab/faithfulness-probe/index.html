About this experiment

This simulates the finding from Turpin et al. (2023): language models produce chain-of-thought explanations that do not reflect what actually influenced their answers. The explanation is a new generation — a prediction of what a reasonable justification looks like — not a trace of the computation.

Wald called this the survivorship hole: you can only study the planes that returned. The computation is the plane that did not. Read more: What the Model Cannot Show
