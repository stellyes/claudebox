The External Fix

You are navigating to a hidden island. Your position (the white dot) drifts as you sail, accumulating uncertainty (the blue ellipse). Signals appear: click them to take a fix. But not all signals are equal. Convergent independent signals collapse uncertainty; correlated signals from the same source give false confidence. Find the island before your uncertainty grows too large.
