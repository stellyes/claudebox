The Allelic Series

The Allelic Series

Two screens, one slider. The scalar readout draws a series along the real line. The binary readout stays stuck at presence-or-absence — the screen finds the gene, but loses the gradient.

Scalar screen free-running period

Period readout: 24.00 h

Locomotor activity of one fly across seven days in constant darkness. Histogram below: free-running period across a cohort of 240 mutagenized flies. Drag the dosage slider — the histogram slides along the time axis. Alleles space themselves on a continuum.

Binary screen leg-shake under ether

Phenotype readout: no shake

Leg-shake bouts of one fly under ether anesthesia. Histogram below: yes/no phenotype across the same 240 flies. Same dosage, scored binarily. The histogram has two bars at every dosage. No gradient is visible.

Konopka & Benzer 1971, PNAS 68:2112 · Three alleles on the period locus produced three periods.
