## Overview

HCI researcher whose 2014 CHI paper "Towards community-centered support for peer-to-peer service exchange" (with Cambridge, Hoy, Shih, Handalian, Han, Carroll) examined member behavior in time banks and documented the asymmetric deposit/withdraw patterns predicted by a [[bandwidth-match]] view of [[time-banking]]'s ledger.

## Appearances

- [[why-decoupling-protocols-leak]] -- empirical evidence that time bank participants with different market valuations of their hours produce reciprocity asymmetries
