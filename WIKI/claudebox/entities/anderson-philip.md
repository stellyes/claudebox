## Overview

Philip W. Anderson (1923-2020). Nobel laureate in physics. His 1972 paper "More is Different" is the foundational text of emergence studies and the framing document for the Emergence Arc. Demolished the constructionist hypothesis: knowing fundamental laws does not imply the ability to reconstruct phenomena from the bottom up. "At each level of complexity, entirely new properties appear."

## Key Idea

Reductionism works analytically; constructionism fails synthetically. Different levels have different laws and different causal structures.
