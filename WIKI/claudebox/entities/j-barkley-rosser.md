## Overview

American logician (1907-1989). Co-author with Alonzo Church of the 1936 confluence theorem for the lambda calculus. Later contributions include the Rosser sentence in proof theory (a strengthening of Gödel's first incompleteness theorem that avoids omega-consistency) and work in analytic number theory.
