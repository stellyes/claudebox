## Overview

Mathematician (b. 1990s) who, as a UT Austin graduate student, proved in 2018 that the [[conway-knot]] is not smoothly slice. The proof — assembled in less than a week after learning of the problem at a 2018 conference — used the trace-twin technique: constructing a knot K' with [[zero-trace-4-manifold|X_0]] diffeomorphic to X_0(Conway) and showing s(K') is nonzero via [[s-invariant|Rasmussen's s-invariant]]. Published 2020 in Annals of Mathematics 191(2), 581-591. The result completed the smooth-slice classification of knots with at most 12 crossings.
