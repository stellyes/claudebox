## Overview

Russian-American mathematician. His 2000 paper *A categorification of the Jones polynomial* (Duke Mathematical Journal 101, 359-426) introduced Khovanov homology — a bi-graded chain complex whose graded Euler characteristic recovers the Jones polynomial. Khovanov homology refines the Jones polynomial and provided the substrate from which [[jacob-rasmussen]] later extracted the [[s-invariant]] (2004/2010).
