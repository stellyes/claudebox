## Overview

French composer and film-sound theorist (b. 1947) who studied with [[pierre-schaeffer]] and authored the 1983 *Guide des objets sonores* — the most thorough catalogue of how often Schaeffer's reduced-listening discipline fails in practice, even among trained listeners.
