## Overview

Kenyan marathoner. World record holder 2018-2023. Subject of two organized attempts to break the two-hour marathon barrier: Nike Breaking2 (Monza 2017, 2:00:25 unofficial) and INEOS 1:59 (Vienna 2019, 1:59:40 unofficial — first human under 2:00 in any conditions).

Official records: Berlin 2018 (2:01:39), Berlin 2022 (2:01:09). His career marked the steep portion of the post-Kimetto record progression and the introduction of carbon-plated racing shoes.

## Physiological estimates (public)

- VO2max: ~78-80 ml/kg/min
- Lactate threshold: ~83-85% of VO2max
- Running economy: ~180 ml/kg/km — exceptional, ~2% better than peers at comparable VO2max
