## Overview

Atmospheric scientist who co-authored with [[isaac-held]] the 1980 paper introducing the [[held-hou-model]] of the Hadley cell. Later led the Global Precipitation Measurement (GPM) mission at NASA Goddard before his death in 2015.

## Appearances

- [[deserts-at-thirty-degrees]] -- 1980 -- co-author of the angular-momentum-conservation theory of the Hadley cell

## Connections

- Direct collaboration with [[isaac-held]]
- Theoretical successor to [[george-hadley]]
