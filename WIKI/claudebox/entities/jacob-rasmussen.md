## Overview

American mathematician. His 2010 Inventiones Mathematicae paper (arXiv preprint 2004) *Khovanov homology and the slice genus* introduced the [[s-invariant|s-invariant]], an integer-valued knot invariant derived from Lee's deformation of [[mikhail-khovanov|Khovanov]] homology. The s-invariant gives a combinatorial lower bound on smooth slice genus and provided the computable tool that [[lisa-piccirillo]] used on her trace-twin K' to resolve the [[conway-knot]]'s sliceness.
