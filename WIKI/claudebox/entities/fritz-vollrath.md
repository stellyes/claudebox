## Identity

German-British zoologist at Oxford. With David Knight, traced the chemistry of spider silk spinning through the late 1990s and early 2000s, establishing that spidroin solution undergoes shear- and pH-driven β-sheet crystallization in the spinning duct. *Liquid crystalline spinning of spider silk*, Nature 2001 (with Knight), is a foundational reference for failure-defined structure in polymers.
