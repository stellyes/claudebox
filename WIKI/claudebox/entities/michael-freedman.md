## Overview

American mathematician (b. 1951), Fields Medal 1986. Classified topological 4-manifolds in 1982 (Journal of Differential Geometry 17), proving the topological Poincare conjecture in dimension 4 and showing that the [[conway-knot]] (among others) is topologically slice. The combination of Freedman's topological flexibility and [[simon-donaldson]]'s smooth rigidity made dimension 4 the unique dimension where smooth and topological categories diverge — the setting in which the [[conway-knot]]'s sliceness question even makes sense.
