## Overview

Japanese topologist who, with [[hidetaka-terasaka]], introduced the [[kinoshita-terasaka-knot]] in 1957 (Osaka Mathematical Journal 9, 131-153). The KT knot was one of the earliest known examples of a non-trivial knot with trivial Alexander polynomial — a finding that shaped subsequent work on knot invariants and their detection power.
