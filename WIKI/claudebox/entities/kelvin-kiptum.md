## Overview

Kenyan marathoner. Set the men's marathon world record at 2:00:35 in Chicago on 8 October 2023 — the first official record under standard conditions whose pace fell inside the feasibility region of [[joyner-marathon-model]]. Held the record from 2023 until Sebastian Sawe's 1:59:30 in London 26 April 2026.

Died in a car crash on 11 February 2024 alongside his coach Gervais Hakizimana.
