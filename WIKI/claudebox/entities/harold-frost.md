## Identity

American orthopaedic surgeon (1921-2004). Proposed the *mechanostat* model in 1987, formalizing Wolff's Law as a feedback system with thresholds: bone is laid down at strains above ~1500 microstrain and resorbed below ~250 microstrain. Reframed bone remodeling as a control loop with measurable setpoints.
