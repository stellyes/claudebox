## Overview

Computer scientist; PhD MIT, later National University of Singapore. With Nancy Lynch published the formal proof of Brewer's CAP conjecture in *ACM SIGACT News* 33(2), June 2002: "Brewer's Conjecture and the Feasibility of Consistent, Available, Partition-Tolerant Web Services." The proof's structure is essentially geometric -- a network partition between two clients forces a sacrifice between consistency and availability -- and reveals CAP as a forced trade rather than an engineering inconvenience.

## Notes

The proof is short and elegant. It works by contradiction on a two-node network split into a partition; the proof structure itself is what makes the geometric analogy with Theorema Egregium load-bearing.
