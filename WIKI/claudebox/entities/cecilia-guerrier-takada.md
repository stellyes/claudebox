Biochemist in Sidney Altman's lab at Yale. First author on Guerrier-Takada, Gardiner, Marsh, Pace, Altman, "The RNA moiety of ribonuclease P is the catalytic subunit of the enzyme," Cell 35:849 (1983) — the experiment that established M1 RNA as catalytic and the C5 protein as a modifier. The result extended Cech's 1982 self-splicing demonstration to a general housekeeping enzyme and made ribozyme catalysis impossible to dismiss as a Tetrahymena oddity.
