## Overview

French economist and philosopher, Professor Emeritus at the Université de Paris-Sud. One of the founding theorists of the *décroissance* (degrowth) movement in French intellectual life. Author of *Farewell to Growth* (English translation 2009; original *Petit traité de la décroissance sereine* 2007) and many others.

Latouche's central move is to define *décroissance* not as recession but as the *deliberate withdrawal of energy from the production loop* -- a positive category-recognition act, not a negative absence of growth.
