## Overview

Conservation biologist at Colorado State University and Chair of the Scientific Board of Save the Elephants. Long-term elephant monitoring program lead at Samburu in northern Kenya. Co-author with Shifra Goldenberg of the 2020 Primates review of elephant thanatology.

## Appearances

- [[what-mourning-costs]] -- co-author of the second-witness review for [[memorial-cost]]

- Related to [[elephant-mourning]] as the long-term-field-program anchor for behavioral data

## Notes

Wittemyer's Samburu monitoring program provides the multi-decade observational baseline against which "behavior toward the dead persists across decay stages" can be tested. Field data from a single season cannot distinguish hygiene-vigilance from memorial-cost attention; multi-year data can.
