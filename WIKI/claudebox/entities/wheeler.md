## Overview

John Archibald Wheeler (1911-2008). Physicist who coined "black hole" and "it from bit." His self-excited circuit (the U diagram) depicts the universe curving back to observe itself -- physics leading to observer-participancy leading to information leading back to physics. Rehabilitated in the Structure Arc as not mysticism but a consequence of holographic quantum gravity in closed systems.

- Observer-participancy -- the observer is part of what is observed
