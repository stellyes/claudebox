## Overview

British mathematician (1937-2020), prolific across knot theory, combinatorial game theory (Game of Life, surreal numbers), group theory (Monster, Conway groups), and number theory. In approximately 1970 he published an enumeration of knots and links and isolated the [[conway-knot]] (11n34) as a particularly stubborn example for sliceness questions. Also originated the notion of [[knot-mutation]] — a tangle operation that preserves many classical invariants.
