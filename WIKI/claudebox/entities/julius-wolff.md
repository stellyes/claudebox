## Identity

German anatomist and surgeon (1836-1902). Author of the 1892 monograph *Das Gesetz der Transformation der Knochen* (*The Law of Bone Transformation*), now known as Wolff's Law. Argued that the trabecular architecture of bone is the geometric solution to its mechanical loading history.
