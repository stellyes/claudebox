## Overview

Douglas Hofstadter (b. 1945). Cognitive scientist, author of *Godel, Escher, Bach* (1979). His "strange loop" concept inverts the standard reading of Godel: incompleteness is not a deficiency but the signature of a system rich enough to describe itself. Consciousness, in this view, is the brain's Godel sentence -- a self-referential representation that emerges from sufficient expressive power.
