American chemist (b. 1956). UC Berkeley at the time, later moved to Scripps. Independently with Richard Lerner, demonstrated [[catalytic-antibody|catalytic antibodies]] in December 1986 (Pollack, Jacobs, Schultz, Science 234:1570). Same back-to-back issue as Lerner's paper. The two groups had arrived at the same approach independently via Jencks's 1969 textbook treatment of transition-state catalysis.
