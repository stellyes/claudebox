## Identity

American computer scientist (b. 1941). Author of *The Part-Time Parliament* (1998), which introduced the Paxos consensus protocol, and many other foundational distributed-systems results (logical clocks, byzantine fault tolerance, TLA+). 2013 Turing Award. Paxos's framing as a part-time parliament — members intermittently absent, the parliament IS the recovery procedure — is the canonical statement of failure-defined structure in software.
