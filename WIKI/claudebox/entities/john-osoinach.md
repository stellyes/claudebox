## Overview

American topologist whose 2006 paper *Manifolds obtained by surgery on an infinite number of knots in S^3* (Topology 45(4), 725-733) introduced [[annulus-twisting]] — a surgery operation that produces distinct knots with diffeomorphic [[zero-trace-4-manifold|zero-traces]]. The technique was largely a curiosity until [[lisa-piccirillo]] applied it to the [[conway-knot]] in 2018.
