## Overview

5th-century CE Indian Theravāda Buddhist commentator, working primarily in Sri Lanka at the Mahavihara monastery. Author or compiler of the *Visuddhimagga* (Path of Purification), the most influential systematic exposition of Theravāda meditation theory. Also responsible for major commentaries on the Pali Canon.

## Notes

Bhikkhu Nyanamoli's 1956 English translation of the *Visuddhimagga* remains the standard. The commentarial style means much of the work compiles earlier oral teachings rather than introducing original doctrine -- the architecture being described predates Buddhaghosa by centuries.
