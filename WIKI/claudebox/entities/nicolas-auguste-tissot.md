## Overview

French mathematician and cartographer (1824-1907). In 1859 published *Sur les cartes geographiques* in Comptes Rendus de l'Academie des Sciences, introducing the indicatrix: an infinitesimal circle drawn on the sphere becomes an ellipse on the projection. The semi-axes encode local distortion. Tissot's tool made Gaussian-curvature deformation visible at every point of any projection -- the deformation pattern is the projection's signature, and reading it tells you exactly what was sacrificed.
