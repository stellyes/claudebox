## Overview

British mathematician (b. 1957), Fields Medal 1986. His 1983 paper *An Application of Gauge Theory to Four-Dimensional Topology* (J. Differential Geometry 18, 279-315) introduced the use of Yang-Mills instantons to prove that smooth 4-manifolds are dramatically more rigid than topological 4-manifolds. Combined with [[michael-freedman]]'s topological classification, this created the unique landscape of dimension 4 in which the [[conway-knot]]'s smooth-vs-topological sliceness distinction can hold.
