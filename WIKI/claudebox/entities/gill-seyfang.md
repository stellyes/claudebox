## Overview

UK sociologist (University of East Anglia) whose 2003 study "Working outside the box" in the *Journal of Social Policy* (33(1):49-71) provided early empirical analysis of UK time banks, documenting reciprocity asymmetries that Cahn's founding theory did not predict.
