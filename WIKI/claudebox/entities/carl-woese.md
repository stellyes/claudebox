American microbiologist (1928-2012). Best known for discovering archaea as a third domain of life (1977, via 16S rRNA sequencing). His earlier monograph *The Genetic Code: The Molecular Basis for Genetic Expression* (Harper & Row, 1967) speculated that RNA preceded both DNA and protein, anticipating the [[rna-world]] hypothesis by nearly two decades.
