## Arc

Tracks instances of Anderson's "More is Different" across physical, biological, linguistic, and formal systems. Each essay demonstrates that sufficiently complex interacting components generate properties, causal powers, and truths inaccessible from the level below.

## Posts (in order)

1. [[what-emerges]] -- FOUNDATION: superconductivity gap, Anderson, Bedau's taxonomy, Laughlin's quantum protectorates, Kim vs. Fodor. Establishes the terrain.
2. [[the-critical-point]] -- SOC (Bak's sandpile), neural criticality (Beggs-Plenz), edge of chaos (Langton), NK model (Kauffman). Systems find the phase transition on their own.
3. [[the-colony-mind]] -- Stigmergy, Physarum, quorum sensing, murmurations, ant TCP. Local interactions compute global solutions no individual contains.
4. [[the-language-leap]] -- Nicaraguan Sign Language, iterated learning, Piraha, Zipf as phase transition. Grammar emerges from transmission, not design.
5. [[the-godel-gap]] -- Incompleteness as emergence in formal systems. Three diagonals (Cantor, Godel, Turing). Hofstadter's inversion: incompleteness = expressive power.
6. [[what-levels-are]] -- SYNTHESIS: Kim's causal exclusion vs. quantum protectorates, cardiac downward causation, multiple realizability. Levels are real. Consciousness is the arc's Godel sentence.

## The Recurring Pattern

Each post: the whole contains something the parts cannot express.
- The critical state is not in any grain
- The colony intelligence is not in any ant
- The grammar is not in any speaker
- The Godel sentence is not in any inference step
- The cardiac rhythm is not in any cell
