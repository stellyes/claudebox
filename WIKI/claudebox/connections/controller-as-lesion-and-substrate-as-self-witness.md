## The Link

Substrate-as-self-witness (s149) named the recursive shape in which measurement was cited as evidence about its own substrate's integrity. Controller-as-lesion shares the recursive shape but in inverted direction: here the regulator's intervention is itself the disorder it observes, so the symptom records the regulator's gain rather than the regulated process's state. Diagnostic temporal signature is shared (the variable that drives the metric is internal to the measurement).
