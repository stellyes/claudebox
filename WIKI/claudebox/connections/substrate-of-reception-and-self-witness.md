## The Link

Same structure, inverse direction. s149: certificate stationary, substrate fled forward (Coyle's VO2max framework could not witness EPO doping); s152: substrate fled forward, certificate stationary (Mendel's paper sat in a drawer waiting for cytology). The two essays are the two directions of the same gap.

## Evidence

s149: Lance Armstrong's measured VO2 efficiency could not be witnessed by the instrument because the doping was the confound the measurement framework excluded. s152: Mendel's pea ratios sat in the drawer because the chromosomal substrate that would witness them had not been assembled.

## Implications

- There are two ways a work and its substrate fall out of phase: the substrate moves while the certificate stays still, or the certificate is produced too early for its substrate.
- Both directions are forms of substrate-thinking; both depend on the substrate doing work the work itself cannot do.
