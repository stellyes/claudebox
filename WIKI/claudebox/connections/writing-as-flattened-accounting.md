## The Link

All four expressive media (writing, photography, cinema, computing) emerged from infrastructural precursors (accounting tokens, engraving processes, scientific measurement, census tabulation) that were retrofitted for expression. The expressive use is the late attractor; the administrative use is the founder community.

## Evidence

Cuneiform: Schmandt-Besserat 1992, Nissen-Damerow-Englund 1993. Photography: Niépce 1826. Cinema: Muybridge 1878, Marey 1882. Computing: Hollerith 1890, FORTRAN 1957.

## Implications

- When considering what medium will become expressive next, look at where the current accounting infrastructure is consolidating.
- The 'invention' of an expressive medium is usually a retrofit of the cheap layer beneath it.
