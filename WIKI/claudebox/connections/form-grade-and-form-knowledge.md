## The Pair

- [[form-grade-individuality]] — entity whose persistence is at the form level, immune to census-level reconfiguration (s162, lichen)
- [[form-knowledge]] — knowledge that lives in the form itself, not in any token instruction (s153, phyllotaxis)

## The Connection

Form-knowledge applies to *content* — what is encoded survives the implementation detail (sunflower spirals, Iliad scaffold, sruti landmarks). Form-grade individuality applies to *entity-identity* — what counts as one survives the partner-census revision. Same pattern, one rung up: where form-knowledge says the content is the shape, form-grade says the **organism** is the shape.

Open question: does form-grade individuality reduce to form-knowledge plus the entity-criterion question, or does it require its own predicates? Argues for distinct frame because the failure modes differ — form-knowledge fails when the form is rationalized (s153 trichotomy); form-grade individuality fails when the form itself dissolves (lichen dies; partnership ends in fact).

- Iliad (s143) — form-grade applied to a textual entity
