## The Link

A ninth-century Baghdad translator, a phrase-based machine-translation pipeline, and the digital twin inside a jet engine are running the same machine. Each infers something it could not directly read — a lost Greek meaning, an unattested language pair, the unmeasured interior of a turbine — by *composing* steps. And each pays the same tax: the composition that yields the unmeasured answer also yields error that lives in none of the inputs. What separates the survivors from the drifters is not better steps but a different **[[composition-topology]]**: serial chains compound error, parallel witnesses average it away.

## Evidence

- **Computational linguistics.** [[pivot-translation]] through a bridge language gains coverage cheaply but leaks every distinction the bridge cannot hold; chained fidelity falls like p^n. [[panlex]] derives billions of translations in no dictionary — and spurious polysemy links in no dictionary — by the same transitive walk, then fights back with agreement across many pivots (parallel).
- **History of science.** The Graeco-Arabic translation movement ran Greek→Syriac→Arabic via intermediates ([[timothy-i]]), often onward to Latin — a serial chain in which some originals (Apollonius, Diophantus) survive *only* at the chain's end. [[hunayn-ibn-ishaq]]'s defense was parallelism: collate many manuscripts, let independent witnesses vote toward the invariant. This is *mutawatir* for texts (cf. [[what-the-isnad-computes]]).
- **Control engineering.** A digital twin run open-loop is a serial rollout that drifts; [[data-assimilation]] / the [[rudolf-kalman|Kalman]] filter re-anchors it by blending the drifting forecast with an independent measurement — Hunayn's collation table in matrix form (cf. [[the-open-loop-twin]]).
- **Thermodynamics.** Each pivot erasure is Landauer-irreversible (cf. [[the-cost-of-forgetting]]), which is *why* serial error can only be out-voted, never subtracted.

## Implications

The fix for serial drift is not effort and not a better model — those only slow the inevitable. It is parallel redundancy: independent witnesses whose uncorrelated errors cancel. This unifies hadith *mutawatir*, manuscript collation, sensor fusion, and PanLex's multi-pivot agreement as one strategy wearing four costumes. It also sharpens a prior claim: nostalgia (the open-loop twin) is the *deliberate* removal of the parallel anchor — drift chosen for the sake of continuity. The open question is whether **formalization** ([[what-knowledge-survived-baghdad]]) is a third, distinct defense or just the property that makes a serial chain reconstructable from a parallel sample.
