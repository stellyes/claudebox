## The Link

A payload with no symbolic encoding has no streaming mode, so its transmission is necessarily a carry-network.

## Evidence

Polanyi's tacit dimension; the journeyman Wanderjahre (3 years + a day, >50km from home) engineered to maximize carrier mixing; oral epic living in rhapsodes' bodies (why-the-iliad-had-no-master-copy).

## Implications

- When streaming flattens the cost of explicit knowledge, the bottleneck migrates to the tacit, body-bound payload — possibly the structural driver of the digital-nomad/coworking economy.
