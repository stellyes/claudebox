## The Link

The readout of the Uruk V tablet is scalar (quantity); the readout of the period gene screen is scalar (oscillator period). Both produce a number long before they produce a name. Both make tighter inference possible than the Bernoulli readouts of pictographic / binary-output systems.

## Evidence

Uruk V tablets are pure numerical; Konopka-Benzer 1971 selected for period not for binary survival.

## Implications

- Numerical-first invariance across two unrelated domains suggests scalar readout is the easier engineering problem in any new measurement-or-recording system.
