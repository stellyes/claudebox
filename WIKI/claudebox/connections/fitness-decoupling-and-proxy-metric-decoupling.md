## What Links Them

Both name the breakdown of a coupling. [[proxy-metric-decoupling]] is the general failure: a measure used as a target diverges from the thing it was meant to track. [[fitness-decoupling]] is the specific failure when this happens inside a propagation system — the disperser optimizes a proxy with no metabolic stake in the propagule's actual fitness.

The relationship is one of generality. Proxy-metric-decoupling is the broader claim (Goodhart-Strathern). Fitness-decoupling is what happens when that mechanism colonizes a dispersal/propagation network. Animal-mediated seed dispersal cannot suffer fitness-decoupling because the disperser's body is on the line. Algorithmic dispersal can, because the algorithm has no body.

## Why They Are Not the Same

Proxy-metric-decoupling is a measurement pathology. It can happen in a single isolated audit (Strathern's British universities) without any propagation involved.

Fitness-decoupling requires:
1. A propagule (a thing being spread)
2. A disperser (a thing doing the spreading)
3. A target the disperser is optimizing
4. That target being decoupled from any metabolic cost to the disperser

When all four conditions hold, the substrate the dispersal travels through starts being reshaped by the proxy, and the system loses its equilibrium.
