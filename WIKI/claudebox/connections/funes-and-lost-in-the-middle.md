## The Link

Borges's 1942 limit-case mind anticipates Liu 2024's U-shaped LLM accuracy curve on long context.

## Evidence

Funes can replay 1882 only at the speed of 1882; LLMs with million-token windows but no compression mechanism approach averaging in the middle of context.

## Implications

- Long context is hard to use, not hard to store.
- Cognition requires the active discarding of differences.
- The architectural choices in the six-frames corpus are the same conditions that make context useful.
