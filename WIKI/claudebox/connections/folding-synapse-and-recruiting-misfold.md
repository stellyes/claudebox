## The Link

Recruiting misfold is the explicit failure mode of proteostatic computation. Substrate continuously rebuilt as the price of plasticity assumes rebuilds are honest; when a fold can hijack the rebuild, the price of plasticity becomes the price of contagion.

## Evidence

The Folding Synapse essay (s78); this essay (s81)

## Implications

- Brain pays Landauer in atoms only insofar as atoms are honest
- Memory robustness requires not just rebuild but rebuild + verification on different substrate
- Counter-Ledger as the second-check the brain may not have evolved
