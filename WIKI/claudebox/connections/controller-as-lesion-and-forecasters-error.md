## The Link

The Forecaster's Error treated motivated reasoning as a willed belief-update that amplifies confirmation bias and corrupts the correction mechanism. The reattending solution recommended in forecasting (consider the opposite; slow down; examine the base rate) has exactly the structure of the dystonia retraining protocol: restore the sensory loop, lift the willed defence, let the corrective signal back into the circuit. Forecasting and motor-control literatures, in disciplines that do not speak to each other, describe the same regulator behaviour.
