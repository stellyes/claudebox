## The Link

What Two Eyes See argued for preserved disparity rather than single-channel resolution. The motor literature on the controller-as-lesion pattern is a strong corollary in a different register: will and attention must remain dissociable as control modes rather than collapse into a single channel of effortful focus. Recovery from stutter, dystonia, or yips requires the two streams to remain distinguishable.

## Evidence

Smith-Weber 2017; Quartarone-Hallett 2013; Smith et al. 2000; sensory-motor retraining protocols (Byl-Merzenich tradition).
