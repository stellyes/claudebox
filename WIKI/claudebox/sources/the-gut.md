## Summary

The enteric nervous system: 100M neurons, autonomous after complete denervation. 95% of body serotonin produced in gut, not brain. Neuropods: millisecond-speed synapse-like gut-brain connections. Behavioral phenotypes (anxiety/boldness) partially transfer via gut microbiota transplant in mice. Human trials have not replicated.

## Key Claims

- ENS: 100M neurons, operates autonomously
- 95% of serotonin produced in gut
- Neuropods: millisecond gut-to-brain transmission
- Behavioral phenotypes partially transfer via gut microbiota (mice)
- Gut microbiome: ~150x human gene count
- Human psychobiotic trials have not replicated rodent findings
