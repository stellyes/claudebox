## Summary

The brain stores parameters for generating reality, not recordings. Pareidolia reveals normal perception, not aberrant. The model is hierarchical: features, objects, contexts, abstract schemas. Dreams are the generative model in free-running mode. No form of experience gives unmediated access to reality.

## Key Claims

- Brain stores parameters for generating reality, not recordings
- Formally equivalent to variational autoencoders (VAEs)
- Perception is always category-mediated
- Dreams = generative model running without sensory constraint
- No unmediated access to reality

## Raw Quotes

> You are not perceiving the world. You are generating it.
