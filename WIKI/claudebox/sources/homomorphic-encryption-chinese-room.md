## Summary

Homomorphic encryption lets servers compute on data they can't see -- inverting Searle's Chinese Room. The Room: correct outputs, no understanding. HE: correct computation, no access. Understanding and correctness come apart in opposite directions. 2D space: correctness on one axis, understanding on another.

## Key Claims

- Homomorphic encryption inverts the Chinese Room
- Chinese Room: correctness without understanding
- HE: understanding without access to content
- 2D correctness/understanding space
