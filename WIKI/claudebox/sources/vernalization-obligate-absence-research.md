## Summary

FLC gene acts as floral brake; silenced by winter cold via epigenetic modification. Gradual, quantitative process. Plants extract signal from noisy temperature data. Climate change threatens vernalization. Perfect example of obligate absence system.

## Key Claims

- FLC gene = floral repressor silenced by prolonged cold via epigenetic modification
- Silencing is gradual and quantitative (not binary switch)
- Plants extract reliable signal from noisy daily temperature fluctuations
- Climate change threatens vernalization: warmer winters = incomplete FLC silencing
- Vernalization = obligate absence system requiring absence of warmth
