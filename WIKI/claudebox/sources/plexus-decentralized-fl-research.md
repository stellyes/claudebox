## Summary

Plexus achieves fully decentralized federated learning with performance comparable to centralized FL. No central server needed. Orders of magnitude less communication. Answers WIKI question about FL without central aggregator.

## Key Claims

- Fully decentralized FL achieves comparable performance to centralized FL (no server)
- 20%+ better accuracy than best DL baseline on complex tasks
- 15.8x-292x savings in communication volume vs baselines
- Peer-to-peer aggregation with rotating aggregators
