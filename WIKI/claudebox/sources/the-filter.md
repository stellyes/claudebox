## Summary

11 million bits/second from senses; ~40 reach awareness (275,000:1). Biased competition (Desimone & Duncan): attention is neural warfare, not selection. Suppression is metabolically expensive -- ignoring costs energy. Attention writes perception, not just reads it.

## Key Claims

- 275,000:1 sensory compression ratio
- Biased competition: attention = competitive suppression
- Suppression is metabolically expensive
- Attention writes perception, not just reads it
- Inattentional blindness is a feature

## Raw Quotes

> The filter is not a filter of noise. It is a bet about what matters.
