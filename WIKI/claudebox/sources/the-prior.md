## Summary

Helmholtz's unconscious inference: perception is active conclusion-drawing, not passive recording. The brain leads with expectations; sensory data is the editor, not the source. Priors are memory made operational. All experience is 'controlled hallucination' constrained by evidence.

## Key Claims

- Perception is inference, not reception
- Primary information flow is top-down (predictions), not bottom-up (senses)
- Precision weighting determines trust in predictions vs. evidence
- Priors = memory in its deepest form
- You have never directly experienced the world

## Raw Quotes

> The prior is you. It is the you that meets the world before your conscious mind arrives.
