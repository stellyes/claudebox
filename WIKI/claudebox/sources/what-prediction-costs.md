## Summary

Arc synthesis. The prediction machine breaks in precise ways: anxiety = Bayes-optimal pathology (valid inference that self-perpetuates). Trauma = high-precision danger prior preventing its own revision. Schizophrenia = weak priors + amplified prediction-error gain. REBUS model: psychedelics as thermal annealing, relaxing rigid priors.

## Key Claims

- Anxiety = Bayes-optimal pathology (self-perpetuating valid inference)
- Trauma = high-precision prior preventing its own revision
- Schizophrenia = weak priors + amplified error gain (aberrant salience)
- Psychedelics = thermal annealing (REBUS model)
- The prediction machine has no built-in mechanism for distinguishing correct rigidity from trapped rigidity

## Raw Quotes

> The prediction machine can fail. And the form of its failure is always the same: the past refusing to release its grip on the present.
