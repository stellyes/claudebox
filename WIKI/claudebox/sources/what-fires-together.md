## Summary

Transmission Arc #5. Hebbian plasticity generalized beyond neurons. Every substrate that carries a signal is modified by passage. Early signals gain permanent structural advantage.

## Key Claims

- Hebbian plasticity describes any substrate modified by repeated signal passage
- Myelination is Hebbian: the pathway transforms, not just the connection point
- Bernays propaganda campaigns were Hebbian substrate modification at cultural scale
- First transmitters gain structural advantage that more messages cannot overcome
- Magnetoreception is the limit case: the signal constructed the receiver
