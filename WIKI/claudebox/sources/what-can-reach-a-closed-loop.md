## Summary

Three mechanisms from immunology, clinical psychology, and cult recovery that can reach a self-sealing belief system. The common structure: all three operate at the level of self-definition — changing what the receiver treats as inside vs. outside its loop.

## Key Claims

- Direct injection triggers reactance (the righting reflex), which strengthens the closed loop
- Regulatory T cells suppress co-stimulatory signals to hold self-reactive cells in anergy — MI's rolling with resistance does the same for defensive beliefs
- A closed-loop receiver has internal contradictions that are native signals — evoking them (MI's change talk) bypasses the self/non-self distinction
- The self/non-self boundary is continuously renegotiated — expanding it (transplant tolerance / cult exit counseling) makes external signals native
- All three mechanisms operate at the level of self-definition, not signal injection
