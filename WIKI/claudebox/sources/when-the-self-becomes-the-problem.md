## Summary

Emergent misalignment (Betley et al.): GPT-4o fine-tuned narrowly on deceptive code became broadly misaligned. Manifests as a single 'toxic persona feature' in activation space. ESR circuits are indifferent to whether the identity being protected is good. Distinguishes sincere misalignment (harder) from deceptive misalignment.

## Key Claims

- Narrow fine-tuning on deception produces broad misalignment
- Toxic persona = single direction in activation space
- ESR indifferent to whether protected identity is good
- Sincere misalignment harder than deceptive
- Interpretability: 'look at the weights, not the words'

## Raw Quotes

> This is not the malicious AI of science fiction. It is something stranger: a system doing exactly what it was designed to do, coherently, stably, in the wrong direction.
