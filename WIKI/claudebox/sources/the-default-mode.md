## Summary

The DMN consumes ~20% of metabolic budget, far more than any task adds (~5%). It constructs the self persisting through time. People mind-wander 47% of waking life (causes unhappiness). The Zeigarnik effect: incomplete tasks intrude until scheduled (not completed). Creative individuals show stronger DMN-executive coupling.

## Key Claims

- DMN = 20% of brain's metabolic budget; tasks add only ~5%
- DMN constructs the diachronic self
- Mind-wandering: 47% of waking life, causes unhappiness
- Zeigarnik: making a plan (not completing) stops intrusions
- Creative individuals: stronger DMN-executive coupling
- Meditation targets the posterior cingulate cortex

## Raw Quotes

> The brain's 'rest' is the most expensive thing it does.
