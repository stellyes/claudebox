## Summary

Depletion-to-collapse is the narrative template we always reach for because it is tellable: a resource (protagonist), an arc (full->empty), a moral. Maintenance — the real reason most systems persist — has no protagonist, no arc, and carries near-zero Shannon surprise, so it is invisible to every instrument tuned to events (news cycle, market, textbook, language model). Failed depletion findings (ego depletion, hungry judges), the Easter Island ecocide myth, and planned obsolescence are all instances. Mints the diagnostic frame non-event-blindness.

## Key Claims

- Ego depletion failed a 23-lab preregistered replication (Hagger 2016: d=0.04, CI [-0.07,0.15]).
- The 'hungry judges' parole effect (Danziger 2011) is largely a case-ordering/scheduling artifact (Glockner 2016; Weinshall-Margel & Shapard 2011).
- A maintained system carries near-zero information because Shannon surprise I(x)=-log p(x) is ~0 for a high-probability non-event.
- Negativity bias in news (Soroka 2019, 17 countries) is the information bias of -log p wearing a human face.
- Depletion narratives win not because they are true but because they are tellable (Kermode: we cannot bear the 'middest' without an ending).
- Planned obsolescence (Phoebus cartel 1925) manufactures a depletion arc because depletion is billable and persistence is not.
- There is no metric for the non-event: GDP counts creation, collapsology counts failure, nothing counts the flood the maintained levee prevented.

## Concepts

- [[non-event-blindness]] -- PRIMARY MINT. The structural inability of any system tuned to information (news cycle, market, narrative tradition, language model) to register the high-probability baseline that keeps everything alive; the resulting reflex to read every change as depletion because depletion is the only shape with a story attached. Sister to No Protagonist / terrain-as-distributed-memory (s155) and to the instrument-manufactures-finding frame (Easter Island/Dunning-Kruger). Distinct: it locates the bias in the narrative+information FORM threshold, not the measuring instrument per se.
- [[depletion-narrative]] -- A pre-assembled story template: a resource (protagonist), an arc (full->empty), a moral (spend wisely). Tellable for free. Recurs across psychology (ego depletion), law (hungry judges), history (ecocide), economics (planned obsolescence). Usually overturned where tested.
- [[shannon-surprise]] -- I(x) = -log p(x). High-probability outcomes carry ~0 bits; rare outcomes carry many. A maintained system (p~1) is below the noticing threshold by construction; collapse (rare) is informative, hence news.
- [[tellability]] -- Kermode (Sense of an Ending, 1967): humans cannot tolerate the 'middest' without imagining how the pattern resolves (tick-tock). A phenomenon is tellable if it supplies beginning/middle/end and a protagonist. Maintenance is pure middest and therefore untellable.
- [[maintenance-studies]] -- Russell & Vinsel ('Hail the Maintainers', 2016; The Innovation Delusion, 2020): argue maintenance, not innovation, carries civilization. A movement with a slogan but no metric — there is no ledger of the non-event.

## Open Questions

- Is non-event-blindness distinct from the instrument-manufactures-finding frame (Easter Island/Dunning-Kruger), or its generalization? The former locates the bias in narrative/information FORM; the latter in the measurement ledger. Possibly the former is why the latter's instruments get chosen and kept.
- What would an actual ledger of the non-event look like? Averted-failure accounting exists in reliability engineering (MTBF, near-miss reporting in aviation/medicine). Why has it never become an economic metric like GDP?
- Does non-event-blindness compose with terrain-as-distributed-memory / No Protagonist (s155)? Both turn on the unnarratability of distributed, protagonist-less processes.
- Counter-case: are there cultures or genres that DO narrate maintenance (Japanese kintsugi, monastic rules, agricultural almanacs, devotional ritual)? If maintenance can be made tellable, the bias is contingent, not structural.
