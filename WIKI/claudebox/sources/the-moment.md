## Summary

The brain constructs 'now' within a window: ~30ms lower bound, ~3 seconds upper bound (specious present). Poetry lines across 20+ languages converge on 2.5-3.5 seconds when recited. The brain retroactively rewrites the recent past (postdiction). The readiness potential is likely stochastic noise, not unconscious decision (Schurger).

## Key Claims

- Specious present: ~30ms to ~3 seconds
- Poetry lines in 20+ languages converge on 2.5-3.5 seconds
- Postdiction: brain retroactively rewrites recent past
- Readiness potential = stochastic noise accumulation (Schurger)
- Three dissociable timing systems: circadian, interval (dopamine), sub-second (cerebellum)
- Time doesn't slow in fear -- memory density increases
