## Summary

Mondrian eliminated diagonals because diagonals imply time (motion, change, becoming). His paintings are visual arguments for the block universe (B-theory of time). The contradiction: the block universe has no diagonals, yet temporal experience can emerge from B-series structure.

## Key Claims

- Mondrian banned diagonals because diagonals imply time
- De Stijl paintings = visual arguments for the block universe
- Contradiction: block universe vs. temporal experience
- B-theory of time vs. lived becoming
