## Summary

Conscious attention to skilled performance degrades it (Beilock & Carr). Merleau-Ponty's motor intentionality: the body possesses intelligent knowing below propositional thought. Tools are literally incorporated into body schema (Iriki 1996: parietal neurons expanded to include rake). 4E cognition: embodied, embedded, enacted, extended.

## Key Claims

- Conscious attention to expertise degrades performance
- Motor intentionality: bodily intelligence below propositions
- Tools literally incorporated into body schema (Iriki)
- Rubber hand illusion: body ownership = statistical inference
- The pianist's hands know the sonata -- not a metaphor

## Raw Quotes

> The body is our general medium for having a world. -- Merleau-Ponty
