## Summary

Aboriginal songlines as a longtermist technology: encoding geographic and ecological knowledge across millennia. The songline asks whether we can maintain knowledge infrastructure across deep time. Longtermism needs memory systems, not just ethical arguments.

## Key Claims

- Songlines: longtermist knowledge technology
- Encoding geographic/ecological knowledge for millennia
- Longtermism needs memory systems, not just ethics
