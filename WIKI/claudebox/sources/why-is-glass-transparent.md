## Summary

Glass is transparent because three independent silences (electronic band gap, phonon mismatch, amorphous structure) all coincide in the visible band. The deeper claim: the visible band is the universal transparency window of low-Z chemistry, the band that abundant Earth atoms leave alone between their UV electronic transitions and IR vibrational modes. Eyes detect in this band because they are made of the same chemistry. The Sun emits there because Wien displacement law puts a 5800K blackbody peak at 500nm. Four-way coincidence that is not a coincidence: it is selection of the observer.

## Key Claims

- Fused silica band gap ~8-9 eV (UV cutoff ~200nm), Si-O phonon stretching ~1100/cm (IR cutoff ~9um), amorphous structure prevents Bragg-scale scattering — three independent silences.
- Low-Z atoms (H, C, N, O, Mg, Al, Si) share similar UV electronic and IR vibrational absorption structure; visible is the universal gap between.
- Eye bulk transparent in visible because made of low-Z chemistry; active absorbers (retinal pi-system) tune downward via conjugation.
- Sun emits in visible because Wien displacement law: 5800K blackbody peak at 500nm. M-dwarfs peak in IR, O-stars in UV, both outside the low-Z window.
- Question contains embedded selection: visible is the label the observer puts on the band the observer cannot help but use.

## Entities

- [[wilhelm-wien]] -- Discovered displacement law (1893): peak wavelength of blackbody scales as 1/T. Key to understanding why Sun-like stars enable visible-light vision.
- [[fused-silica]] -- Amorphous SiO2. Band gap ~8-9 eV, Si-O phonon ~1100/cm, refractive index ~1.46. Transparent 200nm-4um. Canonical wide-bandgap insulator.
- [[rhodopsin]] -- Visual pigment in rod cells. 11-cis retinal chromophore in opsin pocket. Absorption peak ~498nm. Pi-system tuned by protein environment.

## Concepts

- [[low-z-transparency-window]] -- The universal band between UV electronic transitions and IR vibrational modes shared by abundant low-atomic-number Earth atoms. Sets where eyes can evolve.
- [[three-silences]] -- Glass transparency requires three independent absorption channels to all be silent in the visible: electron manifold (band gap), phonon manifold (lattice vibration), structural (amorphous/no Bragg scattering).
- [[wien-blackbody-peak]] -- Peak wavelength × temperature = 2.898e-3 m·K. Sun-like temperatures put peak in low-Z transparency window; cooler/hotter stars peak outside.
- [[observer-band-constraint]] -- A detector cannot be opaque to its target. Eyes made of low-Z chemistry must use the band where low-Z chemistry is transparent. Visible is selected, not arbitrary.
- [[retinal-chromophore-tuning]] -- Conjugated pi-electron system in 11-cis retinal, embedded in opsin protein pocket, tunes HOMO-LUMO gap from native UV down into visible (~500nm). Same low-Z chemistry as transparent bulk, but with delocalized electrons.
- [[anthropic-selection-of-substrate]] -- Generalization of corpus pattern (Vermeer north light, twelve tones, first subtraction): question that looks like property-of-object is actually property-of-substrate-observer-uses.
