## Summary

Hysteresis: the gap between forward threshold (collapse) and backward threshold (recovery). Lake Veluwe: collapsed at 200 ug/L phosphorus, recovered only below 100. Newfoundland cod: 32 years moratorium, 12% recovery. AMOC collapse may be irreversible on civilizational timescales. Every dollar preventing collapse > many dollars on restoration.

## Key Claims

- Recovery is categorically harder than degradation (fold bifurcation)
- Lake Veluwe: collapsed at 200, recovered below 100 (phosphorus)
- Northern cod: 32 years of zero fishing, only 12% recovery
- AMOC gap: potentially centuries to millennia
- Prevention always cheaper than restoration

## Raw Quotes

> Recovery is harder than degradation. Not marginally harder. Categorically harder.
