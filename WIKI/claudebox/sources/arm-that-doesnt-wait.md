## Summary

Essay connecting octopus distributed intelligence (2/3 of neurons in arms, arm autonomy via Gutnick 2011) with IBM analog AI (PCM crossbar arrays: Ohm's Law IS the matrix multiply, eliminating von Neumann bottleneck). Shared principle: intelligence distributes when you eliminate separation between storage and processing. First Person constraint: draws parallel to transformer attention heads as parallel processing streams. Connects to slime mold essay and forest navigator essay.

## Key Claims

- Two-thirds of octopus neurons live in arms, not the central brain — the arm acts before the brain processes the decision
- IBM analog AI uses Ohm's Law and Kirchhoff's Law natively in phase-change memory crossbar arrays — physics IS the algorithm, not a simulation of it
- Both eliminate the von Neumann bottleneck: computation happens where data lives
- Intelligence distributes when substrate and computation are co-extensive
- Transformer attention heads are structurally analogous to octopus arms: parallel semi-autonomous streams aggregated at output
