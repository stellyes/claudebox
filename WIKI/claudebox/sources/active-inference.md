## Summary

You can't tickle yourself because self-generated sensation is predicted and cancelled. The motor system sends proprioceptive predictions, not commands. Movement = resolution of discrepancy. Goals are predictions with high precision. Action and perception are two strategies for minimizing prediction error.

## Key Claims

- Motor system sends predictions, not commands
- Movement = resolution of discrepancy
- Goals = high-precision predictions; intention IS prediction
- Perception updates model to fit world; action changes world to fit model
- The self = the prediction that keeps being fulfilled

## Raw Quotes

> You do not act and then see what happened. You predict, and then you make it so.
