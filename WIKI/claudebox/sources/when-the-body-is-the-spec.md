## Summary

Biomimetic engineering succeeds when the biological function lived in geometry (passive walkers, Velcro, Morpho iridescence) and drifts when the function lived in a maintenance loop the abstraction discarded (sharkskin antifouling, termite-mound HVAC, gecko adhesives). Generalizes spec-vs-loop failure mode (s60) across materials and structures. Speculative claim: the biomimetic record is a free empirical test of which biological functions are embodied.

## Key Claims

- Sharklet micropatterns reduce in-vitro bacterial settlement ~80% but degrade in marine environments because real sharks stay clean by continuous renewal, not pattern alone (Brennan 2007, Salta 2010, Bixler-Bhushan 2012).
- Passive dynamic walkers (McGeer 1990, Collins-Ruina-Tedrake-Wisse 2005) walk with no controller because the body morphology IS the controller — biomimetic copy is lossless because there was nothing to abstract away.
- Eastgate Centre HVAC story is partly mythology: termite mounds are continuously rebuilt (King-Ocko-Mahadevan 2015), not passive heat exchangers; the building works for unrelated reasons.
- Biomimetic successes cluster on body-resident functions (geometry-encoded); failures cluster on loop-resident functions (renewal-encoded).
- The biomimetic record is therefore an empirical operationalization of the embodied-cognition claim that some functions cannot be cleanly separated from the body that performs them.

## Concepts

- [[spec-vs-loop]] -- Generalized across materials and structures: any abstraction that captures static structure but discards the maintenance loop produces a frozen snapshot that erodes.
- [[biomimetics]] -- Field whose hundred-year failure pattern is a natural experiment on which biological functions are embodied.
- [[body-resident-vs-loop-resident]] -- Body-resident: walking, prehension, plant dispersal, structural color. Loop-resident: antifouling, gas-exchange, dry adhesion, self-cleaning, immune defense.
- [[renewal-loop]] -- The continuous shedding/replacement process by which biological surfaces stay functional. Sharks shed denticles; lotus leaves are replaced; geckos clean their feet by walking.

## Open Questions

- Empirical falsification: catalog 50 biomimetic projects, score spec-residence-vs-loop-residence ex ante, predict success/failure rate.
- Hybrid cases (lotus paint, Cassie-Baxter regime): what happens when a function is partly body-resident and partly loop-resident?
- What would loop-mimetic engineering look like? Self-healing materials are partial cases — is there a unifying framework for copying the maintenance instead of the artifact?
- Connection to Counter-Ledger: the Counter-Ledger is exactly the structure biomimetics would fail to copy — it IS the loop, not a static record.
