## Summary

Elephant mourning reframed through the phenomenology of absence. The object of grief is not the present bones but the absent term they index. Farennikova (2013) shows absence is perceived via template-projection-and-mismatch — you perceive the absence of X only if you expected X. Sartre (1943) and Husserl (empty intention/protention) supply the structure; O Connor (2022, gone-but-everlasting) supplies the neuroscience: grief is a prediction that cannot update because the attachment prior is the deepest layer of the model. Yields a law: the felt weight of an absence equals the depth of the expectation it violates. This UNIFIES grief with non-event-blindness (s166) as the two poles of one axis — shallow expectation -> invisible non-event, deep expectation -> grief. Exposes a structural measurement gap: comparative thanatology records behavior toward present bones (the receipt), never the absent object (the loss), because an absence presents nothing to an instrument.

## Key Claims

- Absence is perceptually experienced, not merely inferred (Farennikova 2013 template-mismatch model)
- You can perceive the absence of X only if you carried a template/expectation of X — no expectation, no perceived absence
- Grief is an empty intention (Husserl) that keeps firing into a return that never arrives; the bones are the SITE where the unfulfillable prediction is forced open, not the OBJECT of grief
- O Connor gone-but-everlasting: grief is a learning failure; the deep semantic attachment prediction cannot be overwritten on a normal timescale
- LAW: the felt weight of an absence equals the depth of the expectation it violates — salience is borrowed in advance from how deeply the presence was built into the world-model
- Grief and non-event-blindness are the same function read at its two ends: shallow expectation -> invisible baseline non-event; deep expectation -> grief
- Structural measurement gap: comparative thanatology measures behavior toward present bones (receipt), never the absent term (loss), because absence presents nothing to an instrument

## Concepts

- [[expectation-indexed-absence]] -- PRIMARY MINT. The felt weight/salience of an absence equals the depth of the prior it violates. Unifies grief (deep prior) and non-event-blindness (shallow prior) as two poles of one axis. Candidate 25th corpus diagnostic frame.
- [[phenomenology-of-absence]] -- Absence as a positive intentional object — Sartre cafe, Husserl empty intention; absence is encountered, not concluded.
- [[seeing-absence]] -- Farennikova 2013: absence perceived via template-projection and matching; mismatch between expected template and percept is the experience.
- [[empty-intention]] -- Husserlian reaching-ahead (protention) that anticipates what is not yet given; grief = an empty intention that cannot be fulfilled and will not be withdrawn.
- [[gone-but-everlasting]] -- O Connor 2022: brain holds incompatible models — everlasting semantic attachment vs episodic death memory; grief is the duration of the failure to update the deep prediction.
