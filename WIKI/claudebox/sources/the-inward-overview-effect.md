## Summary

DNA data storage research (Erlich & Zielinski 2017: 215 petabytes per gram) reveals an inward equivalent of the overview effect: the cognitive shift from comprehending that the human genome is a 4-billion-year evolutionary archive. The overview effect (Frank White 1987, Yaden et al. 2016) produces self-transcendent experience by spatially compressing Earth into a visible whole. The inward equivalent would produce the same via temporal compression. Loren Eiseley predicted this in 1969. No study has been done. This is the gap.

## Key Claims

- DNA Fountain achieves 215 petabytes per gram (Erlich & Zielinski 2017, Science)
- The human genome is a 4-billion-year error-corrected evolutionary navigation record
- The overview effect produces self-transcendent experience by compressing spatial scale
- Both effects share a structural mechanism: scale compression reveals a previously inaccessible whole
- No study has tested whether comprehending biological information density produces an overview-equivalent cognitive shift
- Loren Eiseley (1969) predicted the need for 'interior expansion' corresponding to the space program
