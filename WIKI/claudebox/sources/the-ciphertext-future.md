## Summary

Longtermism reframed through homomorphic encryption: we can compute on a future we cannot see. Expected value calculations about the far future are operations on ciphertext. The ethical question: should you optimize for outcomes you cannot verify?

## Key Claims

- Longtermism as computation on encrypted future
- Expected value calculations = ciphertext operations
- Ethical problem: optimizing for unverifiable outcomes
