## Summary

Class named: aphenomenal sensing — sensors whose output is consumed by an effector without entering central perception. Three witnesses: octopus skin LACE (Ramirez-Oakley 2015), bird cryptochrome 4 retinal magnetoreception (Hore-Mouritsen 2016, Xu 2021 Nature), vagal afferent interoception (Berthoud-Neuhuber 2025). Speculation: the integrated central observer is the costly exception, not the rule — built only when the sensor's output cannot be locally consumed. Inverts What the Boundary Knows for biology (boundary knows what bulk does not). Reframes Why the Spec Is Downstream (sensors stay below representation) and Without Asking How (sensing decoupled from perceiving = third bilā kayf instance). AI implication: every artificial cognitive system funnels every signal through the central pass; embodiment requires aphenomenal channels — sensors whose output the central process cannot read.

## Key Claims

- Octopus bimaculoides skin contains r-opsin and full phototransduction cascade in dispersed ciliated sensory cells; excised skin responds to 470nm light by chromatophore expansion (LACE) without brain involvement
- Bird magnetic compass lives in the retina (cryptochrome 4 in double-cone and long-wavelength single-cone outer segments) and operates as a perturbation of vision via radical-pair singlet/triplet ratio biased by geomagnetic field angle
- European robin CRY4a shows greater magnetic sensitivity in vitro than chicken/pigeon CRY4 (Xu et al. 2021 Nature)
- Vagal afferent chemoreception, mechanoreception, and inflammatory signaling carry massive data streams that never reach conscious awareness; corticolimbic effects occur via interoception below the integration threshold
- Speculation: aphenomenal sensors are the rule not the exception; the central observer is what systems build only when local loops are insufficient — novel, long-horizon, or ambiguous-action situations
- Reframe of What the Boundary Knows: in the body, the boundary (skin, retina, gut wall) knows things the bulk (brain) does not — the holographic display whose interior never sees the hologram
- AI architectural implication: integrated central pass is the biological exception; embodiment requires unread channels — sensors whose output the central process cannot read

## Concepts

- [[aphenomenal-sensing]] -- primary architecture: sensor with input and output but no central observer; effector-local consumption without integration into a unified representation
- [[light-activated-chromatophore-expansion]] -- Ramirez-Oakley 2015; r-opsin-driven local skin response to 470nm light in Octopus bimaculoides; effector loop closes without brain
- [[radical-pair-mechanism]] -- singlet/triplet spin oscillation in cryptochrome flavin-tryptophan radicals; geomagnetic field biases ratio; output modulates retinal photoreceptor signal
- [[cryptochrome-4]] -- expressed in outer segments of double-cone and long-wavelength single-cone photoreceptors; ErCry4a more magnetically sensitive than non-migrant homologs
- [[central-observer]] -- the integrating subject; a costly architectural specialization built only when local sensor-effector loops are insufficient
- [[unread-channel]] -- sensor stream that an integrated process is structurally barred from reading; biological default, AI exception
