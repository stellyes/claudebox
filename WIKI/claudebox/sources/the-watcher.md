## Summary

Arc capstone. Opens with Wigner's Friend -- observer inside has definite result while Wigner outside describes superposition. No "view from nowhere" in a closed universe. Traces three frameworks: Many-Worlds (drops collapse), Relational QM (Rovelli: states relative to interacting systems), Quantum Darwinism (Zurek: environment makes redundant copies of pointer states).

Pivotal result: Harlow, Usatyuk, Zhao (2026): a closed universe from outside has Hilbert space dimension ONE -- featureless. From inside, effective dimension ~ e^S where S is observer entropy. "The universe is as complex as you are." This answers Level 4: the observer IS the decoder. The Frauchiger-Renner paradox dissolves when the exterior premise is removed. Wheeler's self-excited circuit rehabilitated: not mysticism but a consequence of holographic quantum gravity. "The structure was always there. So was the eye that sees it. They are not two things."
