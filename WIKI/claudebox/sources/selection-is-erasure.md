## Summary

The pivot post. Argues that natural selection, gradient descent, Bayesian updating, and reading are mathematically identical -- unified by the Price equation. George Price's 1970 equation holds for any population of any things being tracked. Steven Frank (2025) showed it recovers natural selection, Newton's method, SGD, Adam, and Bayesian updating as special cases of one force-metric-bias law.

Selection reduces entropy; by Landauer's principle, entropy reduction requires heat dissipation. Every organism that dies without reproducing is a Landauer erasure event. Proposes: intelligence = high variance-reduction-per-bit-erased. Bayesian updating is thermodynamically optimal. Evolution is wasteful. Recursive insight: selection selects for selection -- evolution shaped brains that do approximate Bayesian inference because it's metabolically efficient.
