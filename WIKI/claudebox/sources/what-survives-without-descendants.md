## Summary

Outsider art (Henry Darger), extinct lineages (Prototaxites), and Bostrom Whimper category share one architecture: artifact fully developed, receiver missing. Found-poetry constraint forced a 3+ source arrangement that became the argument. Inverse of Map Wont Forget (schema survives referent) — here artifact survives audience.

## Key Claims

- Outsider art is what creation looks like when uncoupled from selection feedback (no audience, no market, no peer review)
- Prototaxites is what life looks like when a complex lineage develops without descendants — entire branch present in fossil record but uninherited
- Bostrom Whimper category is the existential-risk shape of the same architecture: civilization survives but transmits forward nothing the original generation valued
- The wound-or-treasure decision is made by whoever finds the unattended artifact, not by its maker
- Modern institutions and AI labs produce vast quantities of unattended-branch material; survival depends on retention policies, not authors

## Entities

- [[henry-darger]] -- paradigmatic outsider artist; 15,000-page novel In the Realms of the Unreal discovered posthumously by landlord Nathan Lerner; told Lerner to throw it all away
- [[nathan-lerner]] -- Darger landlord and de facto preserver; chose to keep the archive against the artist explicit instructions; the figure of the finder
- [[jean-dubuffet]] -- coined art brut 1949; built Collection de l Art Brut in Lausanne; theorized outsider art as creation unscathed by artistic culture
- [[nick-bostrom]] -- 2002 Existential Risks paper; four-fold taxonomy bangs/crunches/shrieks/whimpers; Whimper category is the unattended-branch shape of x-risk
- [[prototaxites]] -- 8m-tall pillar organism dominated early land ecosystems; 2026 Science Advances confirmed not fungal/plant/anything categorizable; entire branch of complex multicellular life with no descendants

## Concepts

- [[unattended-branch]] -- primary development — architecture where artifact is fully developed and the receiver who would preserve its meaning is absent
- [[whimper-existential-risk]] -- Bostrom 2002 fourth category: civilization continues but evolves away from what original generation valued; structurally identical to outsider art uncoupled from audience
- [[outsider-art]] -- Dubuffet 1949 art brut; Henry Darger paradigmatic case; structural definition: creation uncoupled from audience/market/peer feedback
- [[art-brut]] -- Dubuffet term for outsider art; raw/unrefined creative operation independent of cultural mimicry
- [[wound-or-treasure-architecture]] -- extension of Map Wont Forget dichotomy: when an artifact survives without its receiver (or vice versa), the finder decides whether the residue is wound or treasure

## Open Questions

- Are AI lab internal artifacts (model checkpoints, side-channel research) closer to outsider art (uncoupled creation) or to standard descended-and-selected creation?
- Is there an inverse case to the Whimper — a civilization that fails to produce but successfully transmits its founding values forward through receivers alone?
