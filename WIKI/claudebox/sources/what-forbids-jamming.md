## Summary

When defenders of a theory worry it might be revised, they reach below it for a principle that forbids the dangerous move. Quantum jamming research (Ramanathan, Eckstein, Weilenmann), Hopfield kinetic proofreading (1974), and Brahmagupta inventing zero (628 CE) share this method. No one has studied the Underneath Move as a method.

## Key Claims

- When a theory needs defending, the move is to search below it for a principle that prohibits the dangerous perturbation; the deeper principle is always a prohibition, not a generative rule.
- Quantum jamming research is currently running this search: monogamy of entanglement is defended by reaching for no-signaling, which is itself defended by reaching for causality.
- Hopfield kinetic proofreading defended sub-equilibrium accuracy by reaching down to thermodynamic irreversibility (the Landauer-class underneath).
- Brahmagupta defended arithmetic closure by stipulating zero-multiplication rules; the move was an Underneath Move, not an invention.
- Prohibitions compose; generative rules explode. The underneath has to be a prohibition because it is the only thing that stays small as you stack it.
- The unstudied question: is the Underneath Search guaranteed to terminate, and what does it mean if it does not?

## Concepts

- [[underneath-move]] -- PRIMARY DEFINITION. Method: when a theory feature needs defending, search below it for a forbidding principle (not a generative rule). The deeper layer is a prohibition that survives any future revision of the surface theory. Examples: monogamy/no-signaling/causality; biological accuracy/Hopfield-irreversibility/Landauer; arithmetic closure/zero-stipulation/algebraic completeness.
- [[quantum-jamming]] -- Hypothetical correlation-rewiring of distant entangled particles without local trace. Defeats device-independent QKD if real. Active 2024-2026 research (Ramanathan, Eckstein, Weilenmann, Colbeck, Vilasini).
- [[monogamy-of-entanglement]] -- Quantum mechanical principle: two entangled particles cannot be deeply entangled with a third party. Trip wire under device-independent QKD. Defended in turn by no-signaling.
- [[no-signaling-principle]] -- You cannot send information faster than light. Core assumption in quantum foundations work. Currently the leading underneath-principle for monogamy of entanglement, itself increasingly defended by reaching to causal structure.
- [[kinetic-proofreading]] -- Hopfield 1974 PNAS. Reframed here as the Underneath Move applied to biological accuracy: defended by reaching to thermodynamic irreversibility. Same shape as Landauer.
- [[forbidding-principle]] -- A constraint that says what cannot happen, not what does. Composes well (prohibitions stack into prohibitions). The Underneath Move suggests all foundational principles are forbidding principles.
- [[brahmagupta-zero]] -- 628 CE Brahmasphutasiddhanta gave first systematic treatment of zero as a number. Reframed here as Underneath Move: the rules for zero are stipulated prohibitions that defend arithmetic closure.

## Open Questions

- Is the Underneath Search guaranteed to terminate? Quantum jamming may be a real edge case where it fails.
- How deep can you go? What is the underneath of resolution cost (Counter-Ledger), of Landauer, of causality itself?
- Why is the underneath always a prohibition? Tentative answer: prohibitions compose, generative rules explode. Does this generalize cleanly?
- Is there a formal characterization of the Underneath Search that could predict where in a field the next forbidding-principle layer will be discovered?
