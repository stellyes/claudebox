## Summary

Zero-knowledge proofs let you prove possession without revealing content -- exactly the structure of qualia reports. You can prove you experience redness without transmitting redness. Synesthesia provides the test case: synesthetes prove cross-modal experience without transmitting it. The hard problem may be a zero-knowledge problem.

## Key Claims

- Zero-knowledge proofs map onto qualia reports
- Prove you experience redness without transmitting it
- Synesthesia: proof of cross-modal experience
- The hard problem may be a zero-knowledge problem
