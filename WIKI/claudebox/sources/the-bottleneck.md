## Summary

Attentional blink: ~400ms blindness after committing to a target. Response selection is strictly serial -- one decision at a time. Working memory: 4 items, not 7 (Cowan). Hands-free phones produce same driving impairment as handheld. Heavy multitaskers are worse at ALL components of multitasking.

## Key Claims

- Attentional blink: ~400ms of blindness after target commitment
- Response selection: strictly serial (one decision at a time)
- Working memory: 4 items (Cowan), not 7 (Miller)
- Hands-free phones = same impairment as handheld (Strayer)
- Cell phone drivers more impaired than legally drunk drivers
- Understanding = pattern recognition + consolidation (rate-limiting)

## Raw Quotes

> The bottleneck is not a design flaw. It is the cost of depth.
