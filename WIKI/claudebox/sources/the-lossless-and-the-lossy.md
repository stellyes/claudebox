## Summary

Shannon solved communication by excluding meaning. Every compression algorithm embeds a theory of perception. Bartlett's serial reproduction = lossy compression via cultural schemas. The Rigveda: lossless oral transmission for 3,000+ years through 11 recitation modes. Writing is lossless for propositions, catastrophically lossy for tacit knowledge.

## Key Claims

- Every compression algorithm = theory of perception
- Bartlett's serial reproduction = lossy cultural compression
- Rigveda: near-lossless oral transmission for 3,000+ years
- Writing: lossless for propositions, catastrophically lossy for tacit knowledge
- 'What survived is not the message but what the receiver already knows how to reconstruct'

## Raw Quotes
