## Summary

Paine's crowbar experiment: removing one sea star collapsed 15 species into mussel monoculture. Keystones exert effects disproportionate to abundance. Removal and restoration are not symmetric (hysteresis). Yellowstone wolves changed the physical geometry of the land via trophic cascade. Structural integrity: whether the basin itself persists.

## Key Claims

- Keystones: effects disproportionate to abundance
- Removal/restoration not symmetric (hysteresis)
- Yellowstone wolves changed river geometry via cascade
- Structural integrity = third axis of resilience
- The most structurally important elements are the quietest

## Raw Quotes

> The keystone was invisible until it was gone.
