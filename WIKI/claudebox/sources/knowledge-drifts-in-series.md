## Summary

Inference by composition buys knowledge present in no source and charges, by the identical operation, error present in no source. Whether that error compounds or cancels depends on the TOPOLOGY of the composition. SERIAL composition (pivot translation chains, model rollouts, the Greek->Syriac->Arabic->Latin route of the Graeco-Arabic translation movement) compounds error downstream (RMS ~ sigma*sqrt(hops)) and the undo is forbidden by Landauer irreversibility, because each pivot erases the distinctions the bridge cannot carry. PARALLEL composition (Hunayn ibn Ishaq collating multiple manuscripts, mutawatir, Kalman/data-assimilation sensor fusion, PanLex weighting agreement across pivots) averages independent errors toward the invariant (RMS ~ sigma/sqrt(N)). The law: you can only buy down serial drift with parallel redundancy. Reframes What the Isnad Computes (parallelism was the unnamed antidote to serial drift) and The Open-Loop Twin (nostalgia is the deliberate refusal of mutawatir).

## Key Claims

- Transitive/pivot composition manufactures both coverage and corruption by the SAME operation (PanLex derives translations in no dictionary AND spurious polysemy links in no dictionary).
- Error topology is decisive: serial chains compound error (RMS proportional to sigma*sqrt(hops)); parallel independent witnesses average it (RMS proportional to sigma/sqrt(N)). Monte Carlo confirmed to 3 decimals.
- The Graeco-Arabic translation movement was history largest serial-translation system: Greek->Syriac->Arabic via intermediates (Timothy I), with some Greek originals (Apollonius Conics V-VII, Diophantus Arithmetica IV-VII) surviving only in Arabic, then chained onward to Latin at Toledo.
- Hunayn ibn Ishaq antidote was parallelism, not effort: collate many manuscripts, fix defective copies, let independent witnesses vote toward the invariant. This is mutawatir for texts and the Kalman filter for the ninth century.
- Serial drift cannot be subtracted out because each pivot erasure is Landauer-irreversible; it can only be out-voted by parallel redundancy. Open-loop digital twins drift for exactly this reason.
