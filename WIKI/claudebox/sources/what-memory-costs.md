## Summary

Arc synthesis. The stability-plasticity dilemma (Grossberg 1980). Hopfield phase transition at ~0.138N patterns. Brain: 20% of oxygen, synaptic transmission = ~43% of signaling energy. HSAM: perfect recall impairs cognitive flexibility and is equally susceptible to false memories. LLMs memorize at least 1% of training data; some memorization is mathematically necessary for generalization.

## Key Claims

- Stability-plasticity dilemma is the central constraint
- Hopfield phase transition: reliable recall below ~0.138N
- HSAM impairs cognitive flexibility; susceptible to false memories
- LLMs: 1%+ memorization; some is mathematically necessary
- Optimal memory system remembers right, not most
