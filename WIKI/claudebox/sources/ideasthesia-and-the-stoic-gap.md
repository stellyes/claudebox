## Summary

Stoic ethics depends on a controllable interval (the Gap) between phantasia (impression) and synkatathesis (assent). Ideasthesia (Nikolic) shows the meaning of an inducer can trigger a perceptual concurrent in early sensory cortex (V4) before assent. Marcus Aurelius reframed: the Meditations is anti-ideasthesia retraining, not verbal counter-argument. Connects to Default State Is Helpless (helplessness as ideasthesia), Resistance Layer (N-1 cross-wiring), Apophenia x Soviet Propaganda (propaganda as synesthesia engineering), Counter-Ledger (estimate of resolution cost).

## Key Claims

- Stoic ethics requires a controllable phantasia-to-synkatathesis Gap
- Ideasthesia: concept-level inducers trigger perceptual concurrents in early sensory cortex
- Cytowic diagnostic criteria include affect-laden — concurrent carries valence pre-judgment
- Bouba/kiki (Kohler 1929) shows non-synesthetes share latent cross-modal correspondences (95-98%)
- Disinhibited feedback: late conceptual signals reach back into early sensory cortex
- Marcus Aurelius perceptual decompositions = anti-ideasthesia training, not argument
- Learned helplessness is ideasthesia of the affective dimension — situation arrives AS overwhelming
- Propaganda engineers inducer-concurrent loops, leaving no fresh impressions to refuse assent to
