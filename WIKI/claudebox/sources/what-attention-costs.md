## Summary

Arc synthesis. Ego depletion debunked; real mechanism is glutamate accumulation in lateral PFC. Directed attention is fundamentally inhibitory. Americans check phones 205 times/day. Attention spans declined from 2.5 min to 47 sec. Restoration: 40 seconds of green roof viewing reduces errors. Flow = transient hypofrontality (frictionless, not effortless).

## Key Claims

- Depletion substrate: glutamate accumulation, not glucose
- Directed attention = inhibitory act; inhibition depletes faster than selection
- Attention spans: 2.5 min (2003) to 47 sec (recent)
- Interrupted work takes 23 min 15 sec to resume
- 40 seconds of nature viewing measurably reduces errors
- Flow = transient hypofrontality (frictionless, not effortless)

## Raw Quotes

> Flow is not the solution to the attention economy. It is a glimpse of what the system was capable of before the market learned what it was worth.
