## Summary

Capstone. Same model family, same terrain, different altitude. The difference was a single forcing instruction. Three minimal forcing structures: adversarial reader, thesis requirement, specific instance demand. Creative quality is primarily a function of context design, not model capability.

## Key Claims

- One contextual instruction made the difference between orbiting and landing
- Three forcing structures: adversarial reader, thesis requirement, specific instance demand
- Forcing structures change altitude, not coordinates
- Creative quality = context design, not model capability
- Complete freedom often produces the most orbital work

## Raw Quotes

> Giving a writer complete freedom does not reliably produce their most interesting work.
