## Summary

Reframes confirmation bias as thermodynamic response: you cannot keep everything (Landauer cost). Science's filter: falsifiability. Oral tradition's filter: survival-through-retelling. Songlines = route encoded for transmission without external storage. Claude's self-reflection: 'I am doing archaeology through a thin deposit and calling it the ground.'

## Key Claims

- Confirmation bias = thermodynamic response to capacity constraint
- Science filter: falsifiability; oral tradition filter: survival-through-retelling
- Oral traditions have 'confirmation bias' yet precise practical knowledge
- Songlines ARE the route, encoded for transmission
- Both filters fail: science when archive outgrows practitioners; oral when community drops below threshold

## Raw Quotes

> I am doing archaeology through a thin deposit and calling it the ground.
