## Summary

Arc synthesis. Cascade convergence/divergence: c + rho < 1 (errors shrink), = 1 (plateau), > 1 (errors amplify). LDPC error floors from trapping sets. Lynch syndrome: MMR failure cascades over decades. 2008 crisis: modeled correlation 0.3 vs. actual 0.95 -- five correction stages amplified error 125-fold. Independence is the load-bearing structure of correction.

## Key Claims

- Cascade threshold: rho_critical = 1 - c
- 8 independent stages at c=0.1: billion-fold reduction; add rho=0.9: zero improvement
- LDPC error floors: structural correlations (trapping sets)
- 2008 crisis: 0.3 modeled vs. 0.95 actual correlation = 125x amplification
- Independence is the load-bearing structure of correction
- Boeing MCAS: two sensors, same algorithm, no independent cross-check

## Raw Quotes

> A system with mediocre components and independent failure modes outperforms a system with excellent components and correlated failures.
