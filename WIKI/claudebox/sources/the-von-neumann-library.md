## Summary

Reframes Alexandria's death as a design flaw: the von Neumann bottleneck (storage/processing separation). The critical blow was Ptolemy VIII's expulsion of scholars in 145 BC (not fire). ~90% of AI inference energy = moving data, not computing. Neuromorphic computing: synapse IS simultaneously memory and computation. LLMs: weights ARE the knowledge.

## Key Claims

- Alexandria died from von Neumann bottleneck, not fire
- Critical: scholar expulsion (145 BC), not scroll destruction
- ~90% of AI inference energy = moving data
- Neuromorphic: synapse = memory + computation simultaneously
- A scroll is not knowledge; it's a prompt for knowledge

## Raw Quotes

> Processing decides what storage survives, not the other way around.
