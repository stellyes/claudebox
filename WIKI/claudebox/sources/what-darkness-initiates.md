## Summary

Essay arguing that darkness/gaps are not merely backgrounds for certain signals but their operational triggers. Fireflies cannot mate in artificial light because the mating trigger never fires (not masking). The Antikythera mechanism was a machine for finding darkness (eclipse windows). Kirby language bottleneck generates grammar. DMN requires cognitive quiet. Central distinction: disarmament vs. masking.

## Key Claims

- The Antikythera lower dial tracked the Saros eclipse cycle — it was a machine for locating darkness, not tracking brightness
- A 2022 study found 96% of male fireflies preferred darkness for mating; no mating in artificial light; males walked past females without triggering
- Darkness disarms the firefly courtship sequence (trigger failure), not merely masks the signal
- Kirby 2008: transmission bottleneck generates grammar by selecting for compositionality — the gap is the mechanism
- DMN requires the dark intervals of directed attention for memory consolidation, future simulation, and social modeling
- Key distinction: disarmament vs. masking — signal masking is solvable by increasing signal strength; trigger disarmament is not
