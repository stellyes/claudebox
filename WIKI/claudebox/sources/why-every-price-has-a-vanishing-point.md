## Summary

Linear perspective and market price are the same projection: both make a heterogeneous, multi-stakeholder reality commensurable by organizing it from a single privileged viewpoint (the station point), at the fixed cost that every other relation becomes an anamorphic distortion the representation cannot register. Crosby pantometry ties perspective and double-entry bookkeeping to one 15th-c. abstraction engine. Reform has exactly two moves: move the eye (redistribution, conserves distortion) or split the projection (the CLT removes land from the priced surface). The marginal exchanger is the only crisp stakeholder; the 30-year tenant is the skull in Holbeins Ambassadors.

## Key Claims

- Linear perspective is correct from exactly one station point and anamorphic from every other; Alberti finestra aperta presents one eyes geometry as a neutral window (Panofsky: symbolic form not optics).
- A market price has the same property: correct only from the marginal exchangers viewpoint, distorting every other stakeholders real relation to the land.
- Crosby pantometry: perspective painting and double-entry bookkeeping are one quantification revolution (Piero della Francesca + Pacioli, Borgo San Sepolcro).
- Reform has exactly two moves: MOVE THE EYE (subsidy/voucher; relocates distortion, cannot zero it) vs SPLIT THE PROJECTION (CLT removes land from the priced surface; ground-distortion to zero).
- New Communities Inc (1969, Leesburg GA) built by Black farmers evicted for registering to vote = people the projection had no seat for; patterned on Jewish National Fund + Gramdan.
