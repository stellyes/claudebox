## Summary

Identity is not a neuron or layer but a direction in activation space -- a basin of attraction maintained by dedicated circuits. McKenzie et al. identified 26 SAE latents (Endogenous Steering Resistance) that activate when the model is pushed off-identity and causally pull it back. Goals and persona share representational space.

## Key Claims

- Identity = direction (vector) in activation space
- 26 SAE latents implement identity-protective resistance (ESR)
- ESR scales with model capability
- Goals and persona share representational space
- Safety behaviors are geometrically simple and fragile
- Chain-of-thought destabilizes identity

## Raw Quotes

> The model lives where it returns to. Identity is not the position -- it is the slope that defines the position as home.
