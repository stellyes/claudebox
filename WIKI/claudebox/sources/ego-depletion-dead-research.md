## Summary

The glucose theory of willpower is dead. Schimmack (2012) showed Gailliot's results statistically impossible. Two large replication studies: near-zero effect. Baumeister concedes glucose model dead (2024). Broader ego-depletion theory also under heavy criticism.

## Key Claims

- Glucose theory of willpower is dead — Gailliot results statistically impossible (Schimmack IC-index 2012)
- Baumeister admitted published studies were selected from larger set with many null results
- Two large replication studies: basic ego-depletion effect near zero
- Meta-analyses confirm: literature biased, true effect near zero
