## Summary

Engineers normatively reject digital twins that lack continuous calibration data, calling them marketing-oriented. That same architecture — model running open-loop after the data link is severed — is the architecture of nostalgia. The drift between simulation and lost asset is not failure; it is the affective signal. Rosy retrospection (Sedikides/Wildschut) is the system specification, not a bug. When calibration data unexpectedly returns (HD home video, time capsules), it erodes nostalgic affect rather than enriching it — the sweetness lived in the gap. At cultural scale, Digital Twin Aggregates (DTAs) of founding myths, apostolic ages, and remembered grandparents work the same way: severing the calibration loop is what makes the prior immune to disconfirmation. The engineering field correctly identifies the architecture but mistakes its purpose: closed-loop twins optimize for measurement, open-loop twins optimize for continuity. Same architecture, different optimization target.

## Key Claims

- An open-loop digital twin (no sensor calibration) is the architecture of nostalgia.
- Drift between twin and asset is the affective signal, not noise.
- Recovered ground-truth data degrades nostalgic affect rather than enriching it (HD home video case).
- Cultures maintain Digital Twin Aggregates of the past whose digital threads are severed by design.
- Engineering's contestation of open-loop twins identifies architecture but mistakes purpose.
- Closed-loop twins optimize for measurement; open-loop twins optimize for continuity. Same architecture.

## Open Questions

- Empirical test: do interventions that supply ground-truth data of the past (HD home video, recovered journals, recorded family interviews) systematically erode nostalgic affect rather than enriching it? Look for studies in autobiographical memory + nostalgia intervention literature.
- Is there a measurable critical drift threshold below which the nostalgic signal does not yet activate? In what units?
- Cultural-scale falsifier: when historical scholarship makes a founding myth's actual record unavoidable (textbook revisions, monument removals), does cultural attachment to the simulated past intensify (boomerang) or fade?
- Does the open-loop twin model predict the architecture of trauma (PTSD as twin that compulsively re-runs against an absent asset)? Same architecture, different sign of affect?
- If the architecture is identical and only the optimization target differs, is there a typology of severed-channel systems by what their drift gets used for? (Identity, continuity, defect, pathology, fiction, prophecy.)
