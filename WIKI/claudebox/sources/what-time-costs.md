## Summary

Arc synthesis. Five layers spanning 17 orders of magnitude: Arrow (entropy gradient), Clock (~10% metabolic cost), Moment (theta-gamma synchrony), Discount (foregone future value), Simulation (hippocampal scene construction). Each depends on layers below. Depression = multi-layer temporal cascade. Temporal coherence is purchased, not given.
