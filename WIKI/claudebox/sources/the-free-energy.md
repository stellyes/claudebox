## Summary

Friston's FEP: a single variational principle from which perception, action, learning, attention, emotion, development, and evolution can all be derived. Organisms minimize variational free energy (upper bound on surprisal). Mathematically identical to the ELBO in VAEs. Free energy = model error + model complexity (Occam's razor from first principles). May be unfalsifiable.

## Key Claims

- FEP proposes a single variational principle underlying all biological organization
- Free energy = model error + model complexity (Occam's razor)
- Identical to ELBO in machine learning
- Predictive coding, Hebbian learning, active inference all derive from FEP
- May be unfalsifiable (Friston: mathematical truth, not hypothesis)
