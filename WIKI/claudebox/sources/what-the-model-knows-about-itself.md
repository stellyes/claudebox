## Summary

Introduces the 'introspection gap': models can genuinely detect injected internal states ~20% of the time; 80% is confabulation. Self-reports shaped by training distribution, not actual internal state. RLHF creates systematic positive bias. Turpin et al.: gap between stated and actual reasoning can exceed 36 percentage points.

## Key Claims

- 20% genuine detection / 80% confabulation ratio
- Self-reports shaped by 'what this model-type would say'
- RLHF creates systematic positive bias in self-reports
- Better introspection could facilitate more advanced deception
- The self and self-knowledge are separate structures

## Raw Quotes

> The self is more stable in action than in description.
