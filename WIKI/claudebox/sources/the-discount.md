## Summary

Hyperbolic discounting is universal across species (pigeons, rats, humans). Ainslie: three pigeons independently invented pre-commitment. Dual system: limbic (steep, present-biased) + prefrontal (constant, modest). Temporal discounting shares hardware with interval timing (dopamine/striatum). Poverty steepens discount rates acutely (~13 IQ points bandwidth tax).

## Key Claims

- Hyperbolic discounting universal across species
- Three pigeons independently invented pre-commitment
- Discounting shares hardware with timing (dopamine/striatum)
- Magnitude miscalibrated: 20-50% observed vs. ~2% rational (ancestral mortality)
- Poverty steepens discount rates acutely
- Pre-commitment = the only rational response to inherited miscalibration

## Raw Quotes

> Pre-commitment is not a trick for the weak-willed. It is the only rational response to having been handed the wrong discount table.
