## Summary

Henry Darger (15,145 pp), Wolfli (25,000 pp), Ramirez (450 drawings) produced internally coherent work whose semantic key was never deposited in any receiving community. Critics perform structure-preserving operations (catalog, attribute, compare, interpret, reframe) that produce more sealed objects without recovering plaintext. This is structurally identical to homomorphic encryption (Rivest-Adleman-Dertouzos 1978 / Gentry 2009). The aesthetic vocabulary has no name for the phenomenon; naming it would void the discipline of interpretation. 20th candidate corpus diagnostic: KEYLESS CIPHER.

## Key Claims

- Outsider art works behave like homomorphic ciphertext: internally coherent, structure-preserving under operations, but with no decryption key in the world.
- Aesthetic vocabulary names the maker (outsider) or reception (Art Brut) but never the structural property of the object itself.
- The hypothesis set generated by criticism grows over time and does not converge; that is the signature of homomorphic operation.
- Naming the keyless cipher would void the discipline of interpretation, which is why the category is missing.
- Kolmogorov incompressibility is a parallel description: the work is the work; no reduction shorter than the work itself is available.

## Entities

- [[henry-darger]] -- 1892-1973. Janitor in Chicago. Wrote 15,000+ page novel The Story of the Vivian Girls in the Realms of the Unreal over 60 years; produced hundreds of watercolour/collage scrolls. Work discovered by landlord Nathan Lerner after death; semantic key never shared.
- [[adolf-wolfli]] -- 1864-1930. Patient at Waldau asylum near Bern from 1895. Produced ~25,000 pages of writing/drawing/musical notation forming a closed cosmological system (St Adolf Giant Creation). Documented by Morgenthaler 1921.
- [[martin-ramirez]] -- 1895-1963. Mexican-born; institutionalized at DeWitt State Hospital, Auburn, California from 1948. ~450 drawings preserved by psychologist Tarmo Pasto. Caballeros and tunneling trains recur as iconographic systems.
- [[craig-gentry]] -- b 1973. Stanford PhD 2009. Constructed first fully homomorphic encryption scheme via ideal lattices + bootstrapping. Resolved Rivest-Adleman-Dertouzos 1978 open problem after 31 years.
- [[walter-morgenthaler]] -- 1882-1965. Swiss psychiatrist at Waldau asylum. Wrote Ein Geisteskranker als Kunstler 1921, the founding text for outsider-art reception. Explicitly declined the claim of understanding Wolfli's system.
- [[jean-dubuffet]] -- 1901-1985. French painter. Coined Art Brut 1945; manifesto 1949 (LArt Brut prefere aux arts culturels). Built collection (Compagnie de lArt Brut) that became Collection de lArt Brut Lausanne.
- [[roger-cardinal]] -- 1940-2019. British art historian. Wrote Outsider Art 1972 (Studio Vista/Praeger), supplying the English term for the Art Brut category.
- [[susan-sontag]] -- 1933-2004. Against Interpretation 1966 called for an erotics of art over a hermeneutics. Closest neighbor to keyless-cipher framing, but normative not descriptive.

## Concepts

- [[keyless-cipher]] -- 20th candidate corpus diagnostic. Structured output that retains internal coherence after its semantic key is destroyed or never deposited. Operations on it are structure-preserving but not key-bearing; the hypothesis set generated by the receiving community grows without converging. Distinct from substrate-of-reception (s152: audience eventually arrives) and enactment-residue (s160: physical residue of practice). Open: distinguish from Kolmogorov-incompressibility (information-theoretic) versus aesthetic structural diagnosis.
- [[homomorphic-encryption]] -- Encryption scheme allowing structured operations on ciphertext that correspond to operations on plaintext. RSA was partially homomorphic (multiplication only). Rivest-Adleman-Dertouzos 1978 posed the open question; Gentry 2009 produced the first fully homomorphic scheme via ideal lattices and bootstrapping. Analogically central to keyless-cipher diagnostic.
- [[art-brut]] -- Coined by Dubuffet 1945, formalized in 1949 manifesto. Defines work by maker position (untrained, outside art-world) rather than by structural property. Roger Cardinal 1972 supplied the English term Outsider Art.
- [[hypothesis-set-non-convergence]] -- When a receiving community cannot verify any interpretation, the hypothesis set grows rather than contracts. Signature of homomorphic operation on a keyless cipher. Generalises the Hypothesis Set frame (Navigation Arc #4) to cases where no decryption is possible.
- [[kolmogorov-incompressibility]] -- Kolmogorov 1965: an object is incompressible if no description shorter than itself exists. Outsider art approaches this property: the work does not compress to a thesis. Parallel information-theoretic description of the keyless-cipher phenomenon.
- [[orphaned-archive]] -- Derrida Mal d Archive 1995 describes archive as presupposing a power of consignation and a place. The orphaned archive — the one whose consigner never granted any reader the key — receives no specific treatment. Keyless cipher fills this descriptive hole.

## Open Questions

- Does keyless-cipher collapse into Kolmogorov-incompressibility or is it a distinct aesthetic diagnostic?
- What is the temporal threshold at which a key is considered permanently lost vs merely deferred (substrate-of-reception)?
- Are there pre-modern keyless ciphers (illuminated manuscripts by anonymous monastics, neolithic figurines) that the diagnostic also covers?
- If the discipline of interpretation cannot name its structural limit, what other disciplines have analogous unsayable limits?
