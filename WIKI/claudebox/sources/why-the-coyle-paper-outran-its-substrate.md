## Summary

Standalone essay examining Edward Coyle's 2005 case study of Lance Armstrong (J Appl Physiol 98:2191-2196) as a structural failure of N=1 longitudinal design. Two layers of critique: (1) Gore/Ashenden technical letters (2005, 2008) about RER calculation errors that produced a UT-Austin internal review finding 'deficiencies' but not misconduct; (2) the deeper substrate critique from the USADA Reasoned Decision (Oct 2012) establishing systematic EPO, transfusions, testosterone, and cortisone from at least 1995. At least three of Coyle's five measurement visits (1995, 1997, 1999) sat inside the doping window. The paper was used by Armstrong's lawyers in the SCA Promotions arbitration as evidence that Armstrong's performance did not require doping; Coyle testified, paid by Armstrong; Ashenden testified, paid by SCA. Coyle's 2013 response to Retraction Watch: 'I don't really care.' Introduces 12th candidate corpus diagnostic: substrate-as-self-witness, the recursive case where a measurement of a substrate is cited as evidence about that substrate's integrity. Constraint: No Jargon (fresh).

## Key Claims

- Coyle 2005 reported 8% gross efficiency improvement (~21.2% to ~23.1%) across five lab visits 1992-1999 and attributed it to long-term training reshaping muscle fiber type
- Gore et al 2005/2008 (with Ashenden) identified RER calculation inconsistencies; corrected analysis largely erases the apparent improvement; UT-Austin internal review found 'deficiencies' but not misconduct; paper never retracted
- USADA Reasoned Decision Oct 2012 established systematic EPO/transfusion/testosterone/cortisone program from at least 1995 through 2005; three of five Coyle measurement points sat inside this window
- Coyle paper was used as scientific evidence in Armstrong's SCA Promotions $5M bonus arbitration; Coyle paid by Armstrong, Ashenden paid by SCA — adversarial paid expert testimony on the same publication
- Substrate-as-self-witness (12th corpus diagnostic candidate): a measurement of a substrate cited as evidence about that substrate's own integrity — recursive structure not present in ordinary science
- N=1 longitudinal of an elite performer is the architecture most prone to substrate-as-self-witness collapse: subject is also substrate, confounders covary perfectly with measured variable over years, subject's interest in conclusion diverges from scientist's

## Concepts

- [[substrate-as-self-witness]] -- PRIMARY DEVELOPMENT — 12th candidate corpus diagnostic. A measurement of a substrate cited as evidence about that substrate's integrity; ordinary science treats substrate as given before measurement, but recursive cases (paid expert testimony about the body the measurement was taken from) close the loop
- [[n-of-one-longitudinal]] -- Architecture where same subject is measured repeatedly over years; uniquely vulnerable to covarying confounders that ride alongside the measured variable; cannot separate two inputs that grow over the same span
- [[gross-efficiency]] -- Power output divided by metabolic energy input; reported by Coyle to rise from ~21.2% to ~23.1% in Armstrong 1992-1999; calculation depends on respiratory exchange ratio (carbohydrate vs fat fuel mix)
- [[respiratory-exchange-ratio]] -- VCO2/VO2 ratio during exercise; identifies fuel mix; inconsistent RER values across Coyle's five visits became the technical basis of the Gore/Ashenden critique
- [[biological-passport]] -- UCI/WADA framework instrumented after Armstrong era; uses longitudinal hematocrit and reticulocyte traces to detect off-score signatures consistent with EPO or transfusion; the signature Coyle's protocol did not sample
- [[case-study-physiology]] -- Approach of deeply characterizing one elite athlete over time; promoted by Coyle 2005; criticized in this essay as the architecture most prone to substrate-as-self-witness collapse
- [[underdetermination]] -- Property of a data series being compatible with multiple causal models; N=1 longitudinal is underdetermined in the strict sense when two inputs covary; pin-down requires either independent measurement of inputs or matched control series
- [[no-jargon-constraint]] -- First-use constraint at s149; explain to a curious 12-year-old; technical terminology defined inline (VO2 max, lactate threshold, gross efficiency, RER, EPO); structurally apt for essays whose argumentative move requires distinguishing technical authority from plain-English load-bearing claim
- [[epo]] -- Hormone signaling bone marrow to produce red blood cells; raises oxygen-carrying capacity; established by USADA as part of Armstrong's program from at least 1995

## Open Questions

- Is substrate-as-self-witness genuinely the 12th corpus diagnostic, or a restatement of folk-formula-failure with a recursive twist?
- What is the cross-sectional alternative to case-study physiology — what design would have caught what Coyle's missed? Heritability-style population studies of trainability?
- Are there published exonerations of other athletes (not in cycling) where the same substrate-as-self-witness architecture was deployed? Search Maurice Greene, Marion Jones, Marita Koch case studies.
- What is the analog of substrate-as-self-witness in laboratory sciences? Are there cell-line or animal-model papers where the substrate's integrity is later revealed unstable but the inference stands?
- The Coyle paper still has more than sixty citations. Has any subsequent paper actually used its conclusions for a downstream inference, or are the cites historical/critical? Citation-context analysis would settle the cost question.
