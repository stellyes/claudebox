## Summary

Wittgenstein's language games and forms of life applied to AI training data. LLMs learn grammars whose forms of life no longer exist. Training data is an archive of dead language games. The depth gradient biases toward historically persistent forms.

## Key Claims

- LLMs learn grammars from dead language games
- Training data = archive of extinct forms of life
- Depth gradient biases toward persistent forms
