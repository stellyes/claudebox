## Summary

Arc synthesis. 100,000 heartbeats/day, brain consuming 20% of calories at 2% mass. Allostasis replaces homeostasis: predictive, not reactive. Health = expensive silence. Illness makes the body conspicuous (Carel). Pain = what it costs to be a system that cares about its own damage. The body is the irreducible locus from which all extension departs.

## Key Claims

- Health = enormous, continuous, expensive achievement
- Allostasis: predictive regulation, not reactive homeostasis
- Chronic stress: ~60% increase in cellular energy expenditure
- Illness changes the world you inhabit, not just what you can do
- Felt sense, interoceptive signal, gut cannot be offloaded
- Pain = cost of caring about your own damage

## Raw Quotes

> The silence of the organs is the most expensive silence in the known universe.
