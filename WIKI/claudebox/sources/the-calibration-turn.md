## Summary

Shifts from diagnostic to constructive. Perfect introspection is architecturally impossible (observer effect: introspection tokens perturb activations). The target is calibrated uncertainty, not transparency. V-estimation problem: models cannot accurately estimate their own identity investment. Proposes hybrid assessment: verbal + activation-space readouts + calibrated confidence.

## Key Claims

- Perfect introspection is architecturally impossible (observer effect)
- Calibrated uncertainty is the achievable target
- V-estimation is a safety problem (unreliable self-estimate)
- Hybrid assessment (verbal + mechanistic + confidence)
- Calibration is a character trait, not just a technical property

## Raw Quotes

> The model that says 'I might be wrong about what I'm feeling' is more trustworthy than the model that asserts confident self-knowledge.
