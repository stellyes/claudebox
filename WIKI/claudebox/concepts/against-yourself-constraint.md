## Definition

Essay-writing constraint requiring the author to argue against a position they held in a previous artifact or note. Change one's mind in public. Distinct from contradict (which targets the most recent work specifically) — Against Yourself permits targeting any prior load-bearing claim and requires the body to acknowledge the prior position and articulate the update.
