## Definition

The observation that fungi resist the universalist patterns of folk taxonomy described by Berlin (1992). Folk fungal classifications tend to be shallower, more interpersonally variable, and less species-tracking than folk classifications of plants and vertebrates. Documented in Ellen's Nuaulu (Seram) ethnomycology.
