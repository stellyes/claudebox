## Definition

The structural observation that expressive media tend to be preceded by infrastructural systems doing some accounting, measurement, or administrative function — and the expressive use migrates onto the existing infrastructure later, often centuries late. Cuneiform on clay (accounting → writing), cinema on celluloid (measurement → narrative), software on silicon (tabulation → symbolic computing).
