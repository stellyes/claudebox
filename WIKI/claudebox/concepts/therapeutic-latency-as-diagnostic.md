## Key Sources

- [[the-delay-is-the-diagnosis]] -- The corollary: the delay between an instant cause and its delayed effect measures the time-constant of a hidden feedback loop. Latency is evidence of a defended set-point, not of a slowly filling reservoir. You never see the autoreceptor, lipostat, expectations, or opponent process directly; you infer them from the lag. Reframes [[homeostasis-and-intervention]] and the autoreceptor evidence used in [[why-the-spec-is-downstream]] — there the loop is more than its molecular signature; here the loop's time-constant is legible from outside.
