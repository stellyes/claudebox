## Definition

The reported limit of a system that must not fail catastrophically -- a setpoint placed ABOVE its true floor by a chosen margin. It fires early (a reserve always remains), it is a policy rather than a measurement, and because it is a signal decoupled from the substance it warns about, it can be recalibrated, gamed, and it serves its setter's priorities, not the user's.
