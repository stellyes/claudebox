## Definition

The work required to integrate a given prediction-error signal into a coherent posterior. Not the magnitude of the error itself but the cost of explaining it. Cheap-resolution surprises (hyperstimulators) are the diagnostic the Counter-Ledger uses to flag exploit-class inputs.
