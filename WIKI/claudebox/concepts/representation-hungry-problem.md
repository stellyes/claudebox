## Definition

Clark & Toribio (1994): a cognitive task whose content is absent, non-existent, counterfactual, or abstract -- reasoning about a unicorn, a path not yet walked, a saw-cut not yet made. Such tasks require holding a stand-in (a re-presentation) for what is not present to be coupled with.
