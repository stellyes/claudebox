## Definition

Stable adoption of a standard or technology such that switching to alternatives is impractical, even when alternatives are objectively better. Distinguished from path-dependence as 'third-degree' path-dependence by Liebowitz-Margolis. Examples: QWERTY, NTSC, JavaScript, English measurement units.
