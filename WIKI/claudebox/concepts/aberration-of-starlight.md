## Definition

Annual ~20-arcsec apparent deflection of stellar positions caused by the finite speed of light combined with Earth's orbital velocity. Discovered by Bradley 1729 while attempting to measure parallax; perpendicular to orbital direction (parallax would be aligned). First direct observational proof of Earth's orbital motion.
