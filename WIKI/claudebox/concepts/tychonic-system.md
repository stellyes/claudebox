## Definition

Geo-heliocentric model proposed by Tycho Brahe (~1588): Sun orbits Earth; other planets orbit Sun. The only model that fit pre-telescopic observational evidence including null parallax. Survived Galileo's Venus-phase observations 1610 but not the Bessel/Henderson/Struve detections of 1838-1839.
