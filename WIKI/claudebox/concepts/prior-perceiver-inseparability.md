## Definition

The claim that priors are not knobs turned on a separate cognizer but constitute the cognizer. Subtracting a prior does not return the perceiver to a neutral state; it returns nothing. Argued via three witnesses (octopus arms / federated learning non-IID / Islamic ijtihad) plus predictive-processing theoretical anchor.
