## Key Sources

- [[the-folding-synapse]] -- Long-term potentiation has an early phase (E-LTP, ~1 hr, post-translational) and a late phase (L-LTP, hours-days, requires new protein synthesis). Krug-Lössner-Ott 1984 anisomycin block in rat dentate gyrus: short-term LTP preserved, long-term collapses within 30 min. Kandel's CREB cascade in Aplysia (1986-2000) shows the same pattern: long-term memory is protein-mediated. Operationalizes the claim that the brain's molecular layer is computationally essential, not just maintenance.
