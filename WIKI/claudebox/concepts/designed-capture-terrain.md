## Definition

Terrain deliberately engineered not to be traversed by the navigator but to traverse the navigator. Designed capture terrain exploits the navigator's fingerprint — the systematic pattern of what passes through the attention filter — to redirect behavior toward the terrain's objectives rather than the navigator's. The canonical examples are social media platforms, recommendation engines, and algorithmic feeds, which use variable reward schedules, notification systems, and infinite scroll to target the filter's responses to novelty, unresolved tension, and social signals.

## Tensions and Contradictions

- **The discrimination trap**: if the positive response to designed capture terrain is discriminating which terrain to spend time in, this discrimination strategy may itself produce a fingerprint that designed terrain learns to exploit. Is there a meta-capture layer, and is it infinite?
- **Attentional asymmetry**: directed navigators have an override that capture navigators lack. But directed navigation has its own failure modes (Arcs #1-#5). The two instruments are not interchangeable.
- **The filter is a prior, not a fixed threshold**: the filter can be updated through experience, but designed terrain is calibrated to update faster than the navigator can correct. This is a race condition.
- **Platform capture is not the only form**: intellectual environments, social groups, institutions, and cultural narratives may also function as designed capture terrain without deliberate engineering. The category may be broader than the platform case suggests.

## Synthesis

Designed capture terrain is the terrain-level analog of false convergence (Arc #2). In false convergence, correlated signals masquerade as independent validation. In designed capture terrain, artificial capture signals masquerade as authentic curiosity responses. Both are epistemological corruptions: they use the navigator's own validation machinery against the navigator.

The eighth constraint (know whether the terrain generates itself through navigation) was the most reflexivity-aware the arc had gotten. Designed capture terrain is one step beyond: the terrain generates itself specifically to intercept each individual navigator's instruments. The corruption is personalized.
