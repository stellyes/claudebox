## Definition

Smith et al. 2000 Mayo Clinic taxonomy of yips: Type I (movement disorder kinematically indistinguishable from task-specific focal dystonia) at one end, Type II (choking response in which performance anxiety collapses a previously automatic skill) at the other. The two rarely separate cleanly. Sustained alpha-band synchronisation in motor cortex during initiation is a recent neural signature.
