## Definition

Mendelssohn's 11 March 1829 performance of Bach's St. Matthew Passion at the Berlin Sing-Akademie — the first performance since Bach's lifetime, 79 years after Bach's death. Substrate: Zelter's Berlin Sing-Akademie choral practice, Romantic appetite for the religious sublime, large doubled choirs.
