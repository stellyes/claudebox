## Key Sources

- [[the-delay-is-the-diagnosis]] -- Developed from stub: the general failure mode of intervening on a homeostatic system. The regulator senses the deviation the intervention creates and cancels it; the only durable levers are defeating the sensor or moving the reference. Witnessed by SSRI autoreceptors, the lipostat, monetary expectations, and the opponent process.

- [[why-the-spec-is-downstream]] -- The autoreceptor compensation story: SSRIs are slowed by 5-HT1A presynaptic feedback. Vortioxetine added autoreceptor antagonism to the binding profile, but clinical outcomes did not improve, because the compensation loop is more than its molecular signature.
