## Definition

A hierarchy measuring how internalized a system's decoder is -- how deeply the system contains its own interpretive apparatus. Introduced in [[the-decoder]] and completed in [[the-watcher]].

**Level 0**: Decoder is external (Chinese Room -- rulebook outside the person)
**Level 1**: System contains a description of its decoder
**Level 2**: System contains an operational decoder (can reconstruct its own state)
**Level 3**: System's structure IS its decoder (holographic boundary)
**Level 4**: Observer IS the decoder; observing constitutes the accessible interior (closed universe)

## Synthesis

Understanding is not binary (has it / doesn't) but a continuous scale of self-referential depth. The history of life is "the decoder moving inside" -- from environmental selection (Level 0) to chemical memory to genetic encoding to neural self-models to conscious observation. The deepest result: at Level 4, the outside view is maximally impoverished (dimension 1); all richness is inside.
