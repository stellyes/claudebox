## Definition

Two modes of problem-solving. Searching holds a space of represented candidate answers and moves through it evaluating. Settling is a physical system relaxing to a low-energy configuration that IS the answer (the slime mold flooding then pruning to the shortest path). Settling can only reach solutions that are physical attractors of the system.
