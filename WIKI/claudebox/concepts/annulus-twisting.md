## Definition

A construction of [[john-osoinach]] (2006, Topology): given a knot K with a specific embedded annulus A in S^3, perform a twist (Dehn surgery) along A to produce a different knot K'. Under the right conditions on A, the [[zero-trace-4-manifold|zero-trace]] X_0(K) is diffeomorphic to X_0(K') — so K and K' are [[trace-equivalence|trace-equivalent]] and share sliceness.

[[lisa-piccirillo]]'s contribution was to apply this to the [[conway-knot]] in a way that produces K' on which [[s-invariant|Rasmussen's s-invariant]] computes to nonzero, completing the trace-twin proof.
