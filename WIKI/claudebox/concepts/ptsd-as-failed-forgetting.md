## Definition

Hypothesis that post-traumatic stress disorder is what happens when the protective forgetting that normally compresses pain memory fails — the affective trace refuses to decay. Ehlers & Clark's clinical description of intrusive memories as 'retrieved without a context' is consistent with an EWC penalty stuck at maximum on the wrong parameters.
