## Definition

Arthur Koestler's term (1964) for creative insight arising from perceiving something through two independent frames simultaneously. Not recombination (shuffling one deck) but discovering a link between two decks that had never been in the same game. The resulting idea belongs to the intersection, which had no existence before the act of perception.
