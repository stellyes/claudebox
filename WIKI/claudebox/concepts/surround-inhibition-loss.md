## Definition

Cortical inhibitory mechanism by which a motor command for one muscle simultaneously silences neighbouring motor pools. Its degradation is one of the load-bearing findings in focal-dystonia neurology and one of the proposed circuit-level causes of the controller-as-lesion pattern.
