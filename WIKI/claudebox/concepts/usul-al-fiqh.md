## Definition

The sources of Islamic law and the discipline elucidating them: Qur'an, Sunnah (prophetic practice), ijma (scholarly consensus), and qiyas (analogical deduction). Codified systematically by al-Shafii (d. 820). Treated by classical jurists not as a constraint on free reason but as constitutive of what reasoning IS in the legal domain.
