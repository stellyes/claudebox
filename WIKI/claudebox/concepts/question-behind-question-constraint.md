## Definition

A structural writing constraint introduced in [[why-the-conway-knot-took-50-years]]. Each section opens with an apparent question and closes by revealing the real one, one level up. Used to mirror the underlying claim — that the Conway knot was undetectable for 50 years because the apparent question ('is K slice?') was hiding the real one ('does X_0(K) embed?').

Joins the corpus's roster of structural constraints: [[the-witness-list-constraint]] (s136), [[the-census-constraint]] (s137), Footnote-Only (s139), Reverse Chronology (s140), Two Models Same Data (s135), The Mistake (s134), The Inventory (s133).
