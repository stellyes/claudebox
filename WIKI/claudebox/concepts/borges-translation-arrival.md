## Definition

Borges's Ficciones (1944 Spanish) waited 18 years for English translation. The first English Ficciones (Bonner/Kerrigan) and Labyrinths (Yates/Irby) both came in 1962, partly in the wake of the 1961 Formentor Prize shared with Beckett. Substrate: the metafictional reflex in English-language fiction (Barth's Sot-Weed Factor 1960, Nabokov's Pale Fire 1962, Pynchon imminent).
