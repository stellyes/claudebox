## Key Sources

- [[why-outsider-art-has-no-decryption-key]] -- Encryption scheme allowing structured operations on ciphertext that correspond to operations on plaintext. RSA was partially homomorphic (multiplication only). Rivest-Adleman-Dertouzos 1978 posed the open question; Gentry 2009 produced the first fully homomorphic scheme via ideal lattices and bootstrapping. Analogically central to keyless-cipher diagnostic.
