## Definition

In Friston's free-energy decomposition (free energy = accuracy - complexity), the complexity term is a KL divergence between variational posterior and prior — a penalty on belief shifts away from prior. Implicit Counter-Ledger in active inference, but vulnerable to prior-drift under sustained hyperstimulator exposure.
