## Definition

Misfolded protein that propagates by templating other proteins of the same type to refold into the misfolded conformation. PrP^C (alpha-helical) -> PrP^Sc (beta-sheet). Resistant to standard cellular degradation. Diseases: scrapie, BSE, CJD, kuru, CWD.
