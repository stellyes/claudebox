## Definition

Theophile Thore-Burger's three Gazette des Beaux-Arts articles on Vermeer in 1866, naming 66 works (about half held up to later scholarship). Substrate: photography training the eye to attend to light on cloth and wall, French interior-light painting (Chardin re-elevation, early Impressionism), Dutch national identity seeking seventeenth-century domestic-bourgeois ancestors.
