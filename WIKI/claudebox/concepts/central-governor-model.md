## Definition

Noakes's model (1997) that exercise fatigue is not peripheral fuel depletion but a signal generated by the brain, which limits motor-unit recruitment to keep the body short of catastrophic states (ATP rigor, ischaemia, heat stroke). Contested, but the end-spurt -- accelerating when the finish is safely near -- evidences a defended reserve.
