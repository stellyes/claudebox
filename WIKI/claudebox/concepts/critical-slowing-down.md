## Key Sources

- [[the-wall-and-the-cliff]] -- Recovery rate after perturbation drops to zero at a fold bifurcation (dominant eigenvalue -> 0). Read in reverse, it is the wall/cliff discriminator. Veraart 2012 measured it as distance-to-tipping in cyanobacteria.

- [[stereotype-threat-like-weather]] -- Scheffer et al. 2009: systems near a bifurcation recover from perturbations more slowly, with rising variance and autocorrelation. Predicted here as an early-warning signal before domain disidentification (slower recovery from a bad grade; longer emotional aftershocks).

- [[what-the-bronze-forgot]] -- Scheffer 2009 - only distributed systems produce it; integrators do not.
