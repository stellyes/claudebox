## Definition

The pattern by which a forager's inherited safe-mushroom gestalt closely matches a destination-ecosystem toxic species the source ecosystem did not contain. The closer the inherited identification is to the lethal look-alike, the higher the risk that high-fidelity transmission produces a precise wrong answer.
