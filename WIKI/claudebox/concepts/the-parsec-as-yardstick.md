## Definition

1 parsec = distance at which 1 AU subtends 1 arcsecond. The unit is defined by the geometry of the measurement, not by an external reference. Modern Hipparcos and Gaia distances are in the same units as Bessel's heliometer used. Adopted by IAU 1913 (Turner).
