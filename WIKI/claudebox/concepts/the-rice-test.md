## Definition

Laotian and broadly Southeast Asian folk diagnostic for mushroom toxicity: boil suspect mushrooms with rice; if the rice turns red, the mushrooms are poisonous. The test detects no known mushroom toxin and is mechanistically inert.

## Key Sources

- [[why-the-death-cap-defeats-folklore]] -- Documented in CDC MMWR 30(48):666 (1981) as the proximate decision aid in the Sonoma County refugee poisoning.
