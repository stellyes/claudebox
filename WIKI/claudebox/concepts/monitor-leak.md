## Definition

Specific failure mode in Wegner's dual-process suppression: under cognitive load, the operating process (distractor search) degrades faster than the monitoring process (forbidden-content scan), so the monitor's continued search re-presents the forbidden content. The active tagging of suppression IS the mechanism of recurrence.
