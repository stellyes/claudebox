## Definition

Wegner's dual-process model of mental suppression. An operating process consciously searches for distractors; a monitoring process scans below awareness for the forbidden content. Under cognitive load, the operating process degrades faster than the monitor, releasing the suppressed content with a rebound rate higher than baseline.
