## Definition

Mushroom fruiting event whose magnitude or timing departs sharply from historical patterns due to shifts in temperature and precipitation. Examples: the 2016 northern California A. phalloides bloom (early rain, warm follow-on weather, 14 documented hepatotoxicity cases).
