## Definition

A system that solves problems by becoming the solution -- by settling into a physical configuration that IS the answer -- can only solve problems whose solutions it can physically instantiate. Its power (no controller, cheap, robust) and its ceiling (cannot represent the absent, counterfactual, or abstract) are the same property seen from two sides.
