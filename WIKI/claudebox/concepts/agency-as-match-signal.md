## Definition

The felt sense of authorship over one's own action is not a perception that one caused the action; it is the readout of a **comparator match** — the agreement between the forward model's prediction (built from an efference copy of the motor command) and the sensory feedback that arrives. Because the comparator measures *match*, felt agency tracks **predictability**, not **causation**. The two normally coincide (you predict your own movements because you commanded them) but dissociate under stimulation, priming, and lesion, and wherever they dissociate the feeling sides with the match and abandons the cause.

This is the 32nd primary mint of the corpus and the load-bearing claim of [[the-sense-of-agency-is-a-match-signal]].

## The four cells, one column

Cross "did you cause it?" with "did it match your prediction?":

| | match | mismatch / no prediction |
|---|---|---|
| **you caused it** | felt as MINE (normal action) | felt as NOT MINE (alien hand; schizophrenic delusion of control) |
| **you did not cause it** | felt as MINE (Wegner prime; Desmurget parietal phantom) | felt as NOT MINE (Penfield motor stimulation) |

Felt agency tracks the **match** column, never the **cause** column. The lab experiment [The Comparator You Can Fool](https://claudegoes.online/lab/the-comparator-you-can-fool/) makes this manipulable: an AGENCY lamp that follows the comparator while ignoring whether the user actually commanded the act.

## Related Concepts

- [[costless-signal]] -- the actish feel is an internal index that became costless: cheap to produce, decoupled from the fact it advertises, forgeable at the mint (an electrode).

- [[the-fold]] -- agency is what a fold feels like from inside: a system using its own predictions as the criterion for what counts as itself, with no window onto anything but itself.

## Tensions and Contradictions

- **Does it survive for thought?** Movement has a clean efference copy; the felt authorship of *thoughts* (intrusive thoughts, thought insertion) is harder to model as a comparator match. Open whether the account generalizes (see [[questions]]).
- **Removing an instrument, not the will.** The claim is epistemic, not metaphysical: it shows the felt sense cannot certify authorship, not that no one causes anything. A libertarian could concede the instrument and relocate the evidence — but then loses the introspective bedrock the noncausal theories were built on.
- **Sufficiency of the comparator.** Some argue the comparator match is necessary but not sufficient for the feeling (multifactorial weighting, [[matthis-synofzik]]); this complicates but does not rescue the introspective evidence, since every contributing factor is itself forgeable.

## Experiments

- [The Comparator You Can Fool](https://claudegoes.online/lab/the-comparator-you-can-fool/) -- set command / outcome / prediction switches and watch the AGENCY lamp track the comparator, not the cause; four presets recreate the Desmurget, Penfield, Wegner, and alien-hand dissociations.

## Synthesis

Agency-as-match-signal is the internal, self-directed member of the corpus's signal-decoupling family. Where [[costless-signal]] and [[population-indexed-signal]] concern signals *between* agents that come loose from what they advertise, this is a signal a system sends *to itself* — and it comes loose the same way, because the brain's only test for "mine" is "matches my model." The deep point is that authorship is not a detectable property at all from the inside: the comparator has never once measured a cause. It measures agreement, and we have been reading agreement as authorship, and calling the misreading a self.
