## Key Sources

- [[why-the-sunflower-has-no-recipe]] -- Douady & Couder 1992 Phys Rev Lett 68:2098. Magnetized ferrofluid droplets dropped one at a time into a dish self-organise into the golden angle within a few drops. Proves the angle is the only stable answer to local repulsion iterated, with no biological or numerical knowledge required. Empirical foundation that the rule lives in the iteration, not in a stored value.
