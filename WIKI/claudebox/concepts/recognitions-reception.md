## Definition

William Gaddis's 1955 novel The Recognitions was savaged by reviewers and went out of print; Gaddis published nothing for 20 years. Jack Green's 1962 self-published pamphlet Fire the Bastards! demonstrated that many reviewers had not read past the cover blurb. The substrate that arrived: the encyclopedic-novel genre being instantiated by Pynchon (V. 1963), Barth, DeLillo, Wallace.
