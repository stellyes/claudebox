## Definition

Apparent annual shift of a nearby star against more distant background stars, caused by the Earth's orbital motion. Half-baseline = 1 AU; parallax in arcsec = 1/distance in parsecs. Predicted by Copernicus 1543; first measured by Bessel 1838 for 61 Cygni.

## Key Sources

- [[250-year-null-result-stellar-parallax]] -- 295-year gap between prediction and first detection; each null result during the gap was a measurement of a lower bound on stellar distance.
