## Definition

The equivalence relation on knots in S^3 defined by K ~ K' iff [[zero-trace-4-manifold|X_0(K)]] is diffeomorphic to X_0(K'). Smooth sliceness factors through this equivalence: if K ~ K' then K is slice iff K' is slice. Classical invariants — Alexander, Jones, signature — do *not* in general factor through trace equivalence, which is why they can disagree on a trace-equivalent pair even though they are 'supposed to' detect a property that cannot disagree.
