## Definition

A motor control routine implemented in peripheral nervous tissue (not central CNS) that solves a control problem locally. Sumbre et al. 2001 demonstrated octopus arm extension as a stereotyped wave of bend propagation from base to tip — the arm computes the reach into a low-dimensional virtual-joint trajectory without requiring central command of every muscle. Embodied prior in the strict sense: the arm IS the program.
