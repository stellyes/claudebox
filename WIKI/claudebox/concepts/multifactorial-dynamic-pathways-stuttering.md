## Definition

Smith-Weber 2017 framework for stuttering development. Models stuttering as a coordination structure operating near its margin of stability. Multiple factors (motor, linguistic, emotional) interact nonlinearly across development. Effortful focus on a feared word collapses the coordination structure that would have produced it.
