## Definition

Disorder in which fine, repetitive movement training produces cortical map distortion (adjacent finger representations smear) and loss of surround inhibition, so that the cortical command for one muscle no longer cleanly silences its antagonists. Effortful retraining worsens; sensory-motor retraining with rich attention but without the original willed instruction can re-separate the maps.
