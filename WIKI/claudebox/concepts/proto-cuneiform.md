## Definition

The earliest cuneiform script, attested at Uruk in the late 4th millennium BCE. Subdivided into Uruk V (numerical tablets only, c. 3500-3350 BCE), Uruk IV (numero-ideographic tablets, c. 3350-3200 BCE), and Uruk III (more abstract signs, c. 3200-3000 BCE). Essentially non-phonetic.
