## Definition

Memory of an experience (especially painful or pleasurable) is approximated by averaging the peak intensity and the end intensity, discarding most of the moment-by-moment trace. Documented by Kahneman, Fredrickson, Schreiber, and Redelmeier (1993, 1996).
